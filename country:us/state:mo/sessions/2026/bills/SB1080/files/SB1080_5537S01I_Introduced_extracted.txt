SECOND REGULAR SESSION
SENATE BILL NO. 1080
103RD GENERA L ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY SENATOR GREGORY (21).
5537S.01I KRISTINA MARTIN, Secretary
AN ACT
To repeal section 43.265, RSMo, and to enact in lieu thereof one new section relating to vehicle
purchases by the state highway patrol.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Missouri, as follows:
1 Section A. Section 43.265, RSMo, is repealed and one new
2 section enacted in lieu thereof, to be known as section 43.265,
3 to read as follows:
1 43.265. There is hereby created in the state treasury
2 the "Highway Patrol's Motor Vehicle, Aircraft, and
3 Watercraft Revolving Fund", which shall be administered by
4 the superintendent of the highway patrol. All funds
5 received by the highway patrol from:
6 (1) Any source for purchase and maintenance of highway
7 patrol motor vehicles, watercraft, watercraft motors, and
8 trailers;
9 (2) Any source for reimbursement of costs associated
10 with the official use of highway patrol vehicles;
11 (3) Any source for restitution for damage to or loss
12 of a highway patrol vehicle or aircraft;
13 (4) Any other source for the purchase and maintenance
14 of highway patrol aircraft or aircraft parts; and
15 (5) Government agencies for the reimbursement of costs
16 associated with aircraft flights flown on their behalf by
17 the highway patrol;

and is intended to be omitted in the law.

18 shall be credited to the fund. The state treasurer is the
19 custodian of the fund and shall approve disbursements from
20 the fund subject to appropriation and as provided by law and
21 the constitution of this state at the request of the
22 superintendent of the highway patrol. The balances from
23 this fund shall be used for the purchase and maintenance of

25 watercraft motors, and trailers, highway patrol aircraft or
26 aircraft parts and operational costs. Prior to obligating

31 unexpended balance in the fund at the end of the fiscal year

33 relating to the transfer of unexpended balances to the
34 general revenue fund.
