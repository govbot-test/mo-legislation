AN ACT
To amend supreme court rule 33.01, relating to conditions of release from custody in criminal
proceedings, with a delayed effective date.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
Section A. Supreme court rule 33.01 is amended, to read as follows:
33.01. Misdemeanors or Felonies - Right to Release - Conditions
2 (a) A defendant charged with a bailable offense shall be entitled to be released from
3 custody pending trial or other stage of the criminal proceedings.
4 (b) The defendant's release shall be upon the conditions that:
5 (1) The defendant will appear in the court in which the case is prosecuted or appealed,
6 from time to time as required to answer the criminal charge;
7 (2) The defendant will submit to the orders, judgment and sentence, and process of the
8 court having jurisdiction over the defendant;
9 (3) The defendant shall not commit any new offenses and shall not tamper with any
10 victim or witness in the case, nor have any person do so on the defendant's behalf; and
11 (4) The defendant will comply fully with any and all conditions imposed by the court in
12 granting release.

14 (c) The court shall release the defendant on the defendant's own recognizance subject
15 only to the conditions under subsection (b) with no additional conditions of release unless the
16 court determines such release will not secure the appearance of the defendant at trial, or at any
17 other stage of the criminal proceedings, or the safety of the community or other person,
