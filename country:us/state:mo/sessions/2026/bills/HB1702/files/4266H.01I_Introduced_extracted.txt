573.520. 1. A person commits the offense of engaging in an adult cabaret
2 performance if such performance is:
3 (1) On public property; or

7 misdemeanor for a first offense and a class E felony for any second or subsequent

10 (1) Preempt an ordinance or a regulation, restriction, or license that was lawfully

13 (2) Prevent or preempt a political subdivision from enacting and enforcing in the
14 future other ordinances, regulations, restrictions, or licenses that are in conflict with this
