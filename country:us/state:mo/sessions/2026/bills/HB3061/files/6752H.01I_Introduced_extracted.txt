103RD GENERAL ASSEMBLY

AN ACT
To repeal section 536.031, RSMo, and to enact in lieu thereof one new section relating to
Medicaid regulations.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
Section A. Section 536.031, RSMo, is repealed and one new section enacted in lieu
2 thereof, to be known as section 536.031, to read as follows:
536.031. 1. There is established a publication to be known as the "Code of State
2 Regulations", which shall be published in a format and medium as prescribed by the secretary
3 of state from time to time as determined by the secretary of state.
4 2. The code of state regulations shall contain the full text of all rules of state agencies
5 in force and effect upon the effective date of the first publication thereof, and effective
6 September 1, 1990, it shall be revised no less frequently than monthly thereafter so as to
7 include all rules of state agencies subsequently made, amended or rescinded. The code may
8 also include citations, references, or annotations, prepared by the state agency adopting the
9 rule or by the secretary of state, to any intraagency ruling, attorney general's opinion,
10 determination, decisions, order, or other action of the administrative hearing commission, or
11 any determination, decision, order, or other action of a court interpreting, applying,
12 discussing, distinguishing, or otherwise affecting any rule published in the code.
13 3. The code of state regulations shall be published in a format and medium as
14 prescribed by the secretary of state.
15 4. An agency may incorporate by reference rules, regulations, standards, and
16 guidelines of an agency of the United States or a nationally or state-recognized organization
17 or association without publishing the material in full. The reference in the agency rules shall
