Section 1:
SECOND REGULAR SESSION
103RD GENERA L ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY SENATOR BRATTIN.
5190S.02I KRISTINA MARTIN, Secretary
AN ACT
To amend chapter 138, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to property taxes.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Missouri, as follows:
1 Section A. Chapter 138, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto
2 one new section, to be known as section 138.195, to read as
3 follows:
1 138.195. Notwithstanding any provision of law to the
2 contrary, in any ratio study in which the commission
3 evaluates the assessment ratios of the various counties, the
4 commission shall require that no county's ratio of assessed
5 values is greater than one.
✓
