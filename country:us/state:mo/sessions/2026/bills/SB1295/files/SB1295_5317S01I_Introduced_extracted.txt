SECOND REGULAR SESSION
SENATE BILL NO. 1295
103RD GENERA L ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY SENATOR NICOLA.
5317S.01I KRISTINA MARTIN, Secretary
AN ACT
To repeal section 116.050, RSMo, and to enact in lieu thereof one new section relating to
establishing a filing fee for initiative petitions.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Missouri, as follows:
1 Section A. Section 116.050, RSMo, is repealed and one new
2 section enacted in lieu thereof, to be known as section 116.050,
3 to read as follows:
1 116.050. 1. Initiative and referendum petitions filed
2 under the provisions of this chapter shall consist of pages
3 of a uniform size. Each page, excluding the text of the
4 measure, shall be no larger than eight and one-half by
5 fourteen inches. Each page of an initiative petition shall
6 be attached to or shall contain a full and correct text of
7 the proposed measure. Each page of a referendum petition
8 shall be attached to or shall contain a full and correct
9 text of the measure on which the referendum is sought.
10 2. The full and correct text of all initiative and
11 referendum petition measures shall:
12 (1) Contain all matter which is to be deleted included
13 in its proper place enclosed in brackets and all new matter
14 shown underlined;
15 (2) Include all sections of existing law or of the
16 constitution which would be repealed by the measure; and

SB 1295 2
17 (3) Otherwise conform to the provisions of Article
18 III, Section 28 and Article III, Section 50 of the
19 Constitution and those of this chapter.
20 3. The secretary of state shall collect an initiative
21 petition filing fee of one thousand dollars for each
22 initiative petition filed if the petition relates to a
23 subject on which an initiative petition has been filed by
24 the petitioner since the most recent general election. No
25 fee shall be charged if the petition is the first petition
26 filed by the same petitioner on a particular subject.
