Section 1:
SECOND REGULAR SESSION
103RD GENERAL ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY REPRESENTATIVE REED.
4809H.01I JOSEPH ENGLER, Chief Clerk
AN ACT
To amend chapter 324, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to the issuance of
professional licenses to certain individuals.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:

Section 2:
Section A. Chapter 324, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto one new section, to be
2 known as section 324.1173, to read as follows:

Section 3:
324.1173. 1. Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, for
2 purposes of obtaining a professional or occupational license, a person has demonstrated
3 lawful presence and is eligible to obtain a professional or occupational license if the
4 person submits:
5 (1) An unexpired employment authorization document issued by the United
6 States Department of Homeland Security, Form I-766; and
7 (2) Documentation issued by the United States Department of Homeland
8 Security, the United States Citizenship and Immigration Services, or any other federal
9 agency, including a Form I-797 used by the United States Citizenship and Immigration
10 Services, demonstrating that such person is described in Section 202(c)(2)(B)(i) through
11 (ix) of the federal REAL ID Act of 2005, Pub. L. 109-13.
12 2. A professional or occupational license issued to a person eligible pursuant to
13 subsection 1 of this section shall be valid only for the period of time during which such
14 person's employment authorization document is valid.
15 3. Nothing in this section shall affect the requirements to obtain a professional or
16 occupational license that are unrelated to the lawful presence requirements
17 demonstrated pursuant to this section.
EXPLANATION — Matter enclosed in bold-faced brackets [thus] in the above bill is not enacted and is
intended to be omitted from the law. Matter in bold-face type in the above bill is proposed language.
