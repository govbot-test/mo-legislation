SECOND REGULAR SESSION
HOUSE BILL NO. 2583
103RD GENERAL ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY REPRESENTATIVE TITUS.
5625H.01I JOSEPH ENGLER, Chief Clerk
AN ACT
To amend chapter 226, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to speed limits on
interstate highways.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
Section A. Chapter 226, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto one new section, to be
2 known as section 226.1215, to read as follows:
226.1215. 1. The department of transportation shall conduct a study on
2 increasing the speed limit to seventy-five miles per hour on roadways within the
3 interstate system and report the findings of that study no later than August 28, 2028, to
4 the governor, the speaker of the house of representatives, and the president pro tempore
5 of the senate.
6 2. Any concerns or objections raised in the report relating to increasing the
7 speed limit shall be accompanied by a discussion of potential ways to mitigate such
8 concerns or objections.
9 3. For purposes of this section, "interstate system" means that portion of the
10 national system of interstate and defense highways located within the boundaries of
11 Missouri, as officially designated or as may be hereafter designated by the state
12 highways and transportation commission with the approval of the Secretary of
13 Commerce, pursuant to Title 23, United States Code, as amended.
