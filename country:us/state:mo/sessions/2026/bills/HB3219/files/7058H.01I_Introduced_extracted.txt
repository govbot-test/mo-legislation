Section 1:
SECOND REGULAR SESSION
103RD GENERAL ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY REPRESENTATIVE VAN SCHOIACK.
7058H.01I JOSEPH ENGLER, Chief Clerk
AN ACT
To amend chapter 572, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to the offense of
operation of a video lottery terminal, with penalty provisions.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:

Section 2:
Section A. Chapter 572, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto one new section, to be
2 known as section 572.075, to read as follows:

Section 3:
572.075. 1. A person commits the offense of operation of a video lottery terminal
2 if he or she owns, rents, or leases video lottery games and video lottery game terminals.
3 For the purposes of this section, a "video lottery game terminal" is any device that,
4 through an electronic, mechanical, or electromechanical process, including through the
5 use of any application, software, or other system that accepts legal tender, credits or
6 tickets, or forms of digital currency to play, and through which is used to generate a
7 prize of money or form of credit that can be redeemed for money or another form of
8 monetary payment that is presented to the player by a set or combination of
9 alphanumeric symbols or lines of symbols that determine the amount of the prize,
10 regardless of the operating system that determined the prize.
11 2. The offense of operation of a video lottery terminal is a class E felony.
12 3. The provisions of this section shall not apply to excursion gambling boats
13 licensed by the Missouri gaming commission under sections 313.800 to 313.850.
✔
EXPLANATION — Matter enclosed in bold-faced brackets [thus] in the above bill is not enacted and is
intended to be omitted from the law. Matter in bold-face type in the above bill is proposed language.
