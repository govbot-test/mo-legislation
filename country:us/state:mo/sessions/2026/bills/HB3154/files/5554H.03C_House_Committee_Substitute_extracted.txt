AN ACT
To amend chapter 313, RSMo, by adding thereto two new sections relating to criminal
background checks.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:

Section 2:
Section A. Chapter 313, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto two new sections, to be
2 known as sections 313.912 and 313.1000, to read as follows:

Section 3:
313.912. 1. As used in this section, the following terms mean:
2 (1) "Commission", the Missouri gaming commission;
3 (2) "Fantasy sports contest operator", a sole proprietorship, corporation,
4 partnership, limited liability company, or a division thereof that is engaged in the
5 business of professionally conducting paid fantasy sports contests, as defined under
6 section 313.905, for cash or other prizes or awards for members of the general public
7 that requires cash or cash equivalent as an entry fee to be paid by a member of the
8 general public.
9 2. The commission may require that fingerprint submissions be made as part of
10 an application for a fantasy sports contest operator license by any employees, officers,
11 or directors of a fantasy sports contest operator.
12 3. If the commission requires that fingerprint submissions be made as part of
13 such application, the commission shall require applicants to submit fingerprints to the
14 Missouri state highway patrol for the purpose of conducting a state and federal
15 fingerprint-based criminal history background check.
16 4. The fingerprints and any required fees shall be sent to the Missouri state
17 highway patrol's central repository. The fingerprints shall be used for searching the
18 state criminal records repository and shall also be forwarded to the Federal Bureau of

313.1000. 1. As used in this section, the following terms mean:
2 (1) "Commission", the Missouri gaming commission;
3 (2) "Excursion gambling boat", the same meaning as such term is defined under
4 section 313.800;
5 (3) "Mobile sports wagering license", a license granted by the Missouri gaming
6 commission authorizing the licensee to offer sports wagering through an online sports
7 wagering platform to individuals physically located in this state;
8 (4) "Retail sports wagering license", a license granted by the Missouri gaming
9 commission authorizing the licensee to offer sports wagering in person to individuals at
10 excursion gambling boats or at any location within each sports district, as approved by
11 each applicable professional sports team that plays its home games in such sports
12 district;
13 (5) "Sports district", the premises of a facility located in this state with a
14 capacity of eleven thousand five hundred people or more, at which one or more
15 professional sports teams play their home games, and the surrounding area within four
16 hundred yards of such premises.
17 2. The commission may require that fingerprint submissions be made as part of
18 an application for a retail sports wagering license or mobile sports wagering license.
19 3. If the commission requires that fingerprint submissions be made as part of
20 such application, the commission shall require applicants to submit fingerprints to the
21 Missouri state highway patrol for the purpose of conducting a state and federal
22 fingerprint-based criminal history background check.
23 4. The fingerprints and any required fees shall be sent to the Missouri state
24 highway patrol's central repository. The fingerprints shall be used for searching the
25 state criminal records repository and shall also be forwarded to the Federal Bureau of
26 Investigation for a federal criminal records search under section 43.540. The Missouri
27 state highway patrol shall notify the commission of any criminal history record
28 information or lack of criminal history record information discovered on the individual.
29 Notwithstanding the provisions of section 610.120 to the contrary, all records related to
