Section 1:
SECOND REGULAR SESSION
103RD GENERA L ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY SENATOR BRATTIN.
5961S.01I KRISTINA MARTIN, Secretary
AN ACT
To amend chapter 171, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to tutoring programs
and services.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Missouri, as follows:
1 Section A. Chapter 171, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto
2 one new section, to be known as section 171.216, to read as
3 follows:
1 171.216. 1. Each school district and charter school
2 that currently uses, has used within the past twelve months,
3 or in the future intends to use a tutoring program or
4 service with at least a fifty percent ownership interest
5 held outside the United States shall notify the parent of
6 each student enrolled in such school district or charter
7 school.
8 2. Such notice shall contain:
9 (1) The name of the tutoring program or service;
10 (2) A disclosure of the owner holding a majority
11 ownership interest in such tutoring program or service; and
12 (3) Whether such owner is designated as a foreign
13 adversary of the United States pursuant to 15 CFR 791.4 as
14 of the first day of the school year in which the notice is
15 sent to such parent.
✓
