SECOND REGULAR SESSION
HOUSE BILL NO. 1931
103RD GENERAL ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY REPRESENTATIVE SCHMIDT.
4192H.01I JOSEPH ENGLER, Chief Clerk
AN ACT
To amend chapter 544, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to the
confidentiality of arrest warrants.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
Section A. Chapter 544, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto one new section, to be
2 known as section 544.076, to read as follows:
544.076. 1. Notwithstanding any provision of law and unless determined
2 otherwise by a court, an arrest warrant issued for a criminal offense committed in this
3 state shall not be accessible through an electronic case management system until the
4 arrest warrant has been executed.
5 2. The provisions of this section amend court operating rule 2.04 relating to
6 access to case records, and the provisions of this section shall govern if there is a conflict
7 between this section and court operating rule 2.04 as it relates to the confidentiality of
8 arrest warrant information.
