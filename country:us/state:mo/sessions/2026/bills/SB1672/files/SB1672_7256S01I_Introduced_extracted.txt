SECOND REGULAR SESSION
SENATE BILL NO. 1672
103RD GENERA L ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY SENATOR BROWN (16).
7256S.01I KRISTINA MARTIN, Secretary
AN ACT
To amend chapter 334, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to the practice of
medicine.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Missouri, as follows:
1 Section A. Chapter 334, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto
2 one new section, to be known as section 334.011, to read as
3 follows:
1 334.011. 1. The "practice of medicine" shall mean the
2 examination, evaluation, diagnosis, treatment, operation,
3 prescription, recommendation, prevention, cure, dispensing,
4 or surgery for any human injury, disease, pain, deformity,
5 defect, wound, fracture, infirmity, ailment, or physical or
6 mental condition, real or imaginary, including the
7 management of pregnancy and parturition or cosmetic surgery,
8 whether for compensation or not, and shall also include:
9 (1) Evaluating a patient or rendering an oral,
10 written, or otherwise documented medical opinion, or when
11 providing testimony or records for the purpose of any civil
12 or criminal action before any judicial or administrative
13 proceeding of this state or other forum in this state;
14 (2) The review, oversight, and rendering of decisions
15 on the health care to be provided or not to be provided by a
16 treating physician based upon the treating physician's
17 examination, evaluation, diagnosis, treatment, operation,
