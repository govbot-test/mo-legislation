103RD GENERAL ASSEMBLY

5053H.01I JOSEPH ENGLER, Chief Clerk
AN ACT
To amend chapters 160 and 173, RSMo, by adding thereto two new sections relating to state
funding of educational institutions.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
Section A. Chapters 160 and 173, RSMo, are amended by adding thereto two new
2 sections, to be known as sections 160.2560 and 173.2560, to read as follows:
160.2560. 1. This section shall be known and may be cited as the "Defunding
2 Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion in Elementary and Secondary Education Act".
3 2. As used in this section, the following terms mean:
4 (1) "Department", the state department of elementary and secondary education;
5 (2) "Diversity, equity, and inclusion" or "DEI", any educational program,
6 course, initiative, or curriculum designed to promote, teach, or enforce principles
7 related to diversity, equity, and inclusion. DEI includes, but is not limited to, training,
8 workshops, curriculum materials, student groups, and any other program or event
9 related to such subjects;
10 (3) "Educational institution", each public school and school district in this state;
11 (4) "State funding", any financial support, grants, or allocations provided by the
12 state of Missouri to an educational institution, including federal funds passed through
13 the state.
14 3. (1) No educational institution shall use state funding for the implementation,
15 teaching, or support of DEI programs or initiatives.
EXPLANATION — Matter enclosed in bold-faced brackets [thus] in the above bill is not enacted and is
