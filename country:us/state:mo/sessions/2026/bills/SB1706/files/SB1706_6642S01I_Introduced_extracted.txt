Section 1:
SECOND REGULAR SESSION
103RD GENERA L ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY SENATOR LEWIS.
6642S.01I KRISTINA MARTIN, Secretary
AN ACT
To repeal section 84.730, RSMo, and to enact in lieu thereof one new section relating to funding
for the Kansas City police department.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Missouri, as follows:
1 Section A. Section 84.730, RSMo, is repealed and one new
2 section enacted in lieu thereof, to be known as section 84.730,
3 to read as follows:
1 84.730. 1. (1) It shall be the duty of the board, on
2 the fifteenth day of January of each year, to prepare, in
3 writing, a budget estimating the sum of money which will be
4 necessary for the next fiscal year, to enable the board to
5 discharge the duties hereby imposed upon it, and to meet the
6 expenses of the police department, which it shall forthwith
7 certify to the governing body of such cities, and the budget
8 shall itemize purposes of expenditure by organization units,
9 activities, functions, and character classes in not less
10 detail than "personal services", "contractual services",
11 "commodities", and "capital outlays", and shall in any event
12 be prepared in form and detail similar to the form and
13 detail in which budgets for the various departments of such
14 city government are prepared. The governing body of the
15 cities is hereby required to appropriate the total amount so
16 certified, payable out of the revenue of the cities after
17 first having deducted the amount necessary to pay the
EXPLANATION-Matter enclosed in bold-faced brackets [thus] in this bill is not enacted
and is intended to be omitted in the law.
SB 1706 2
18 interest on the indebtedness of the cities, the amount
19 necessary for lighting the city, and any sum required by law
20 to be placed to the credit of the sinking fund of the
21 cities[, and].
22 (2) If the board shall be required to call out extra
23 police force and the expense thereof shall not have been
24 contemplated in their estimate for the fiscal year during
25 which the extra police force is called out, it shall
26 immediately certify the expense of such additional force,
27 and the additional amount so required shall be appropriated
28 for that purpose, except that in no event shall the
29 governing body of the cities be required to appropriate for
30 the use of the police board in any fiscal year an amount in
31 excess of one-fourth of the general revenue fund of such
32 year.
33 2. The general assembly shall appropriate for each
34 fiscal year an amount of moneys equal to the amount that was
35 appropriated by any such city in the city's previous fiscal
36 year for use of the police board to fund the police
37 department of such city.
