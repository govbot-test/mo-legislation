5 office if:
6 (1) He or she is convicted under the laws of this
7 state of a felony or under the laws of another jurisdiction

9 be a felony, or he or she pleads guilty or nolo contendere
10 of such an offense; or
11 (2) He or she is convicted of or pleads guilty or nolo
12 contendere to an offense involving misconduct in office, or
13 dishonesty; or
14 (3) The Constitution or a statute other than the code
15 so provides.
SB 1102 2

8 exercise of the right of suffrage shall be forever
