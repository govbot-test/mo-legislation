AN ACT
To repeal section 130.034, RSMo, and to enact in lieu thereof one new section relating to
permissible uses for campaign funds.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Missouri, as follows:
1 Section A. Section 130.034, RSMo, is repealed and one new
2 section enacted in lieu thereof, to be known as section 130.034,
3 to read as follows:
