HOUSE BILL NO. 1933
103RD GENERAL ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY REPRESENTATIVE SMITH (74).
4876H.01I JOSEPH ENGLER, Chief Clerk
AN ACT
To amend chapter 290, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to the use of credit
scores by employers.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
Section A. Chapter 290, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto one new section, to be
2 known as section 290.146, to read as follows:
290.146. It shall be an improper employment practice for an employer to
2 discharge, to refuse to hire, or to otherwise disadvantage any individual with respect to
3 compensation, terms, or conditions of employment based on the individual's credit score
4 or credit history unless such individual's credit score or credit history is directly,
5 materially, and substantially related to the duties and performance of the employee or
6 the overall operation of the employer's business.
