HOUSE BILL NO. 2240
103RD GENERAL ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY REPRESENTATIVE PLANK.
4690H.01I JOSEPH ENGLER, Chief Clerk
AN ACT
To repeal section 304.012, RSMo, and to enact in lieu thereof two new sections relating to
autonomous vehicles, with a penalty provision.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
Section A. Section 304.012, RSMo, is repealed and two new sections enacted in lieu
2 thereof, to be known as sections 304.012 and 304.975, to read as follows:
304.012. 1. Every person operating a motor vehicle on the roads and highways of this
2 state shall drive the vehicle in a careful and prudent manner and at a rate of speed so as not to
3 endanger the property of another or the life or limb of any person and shall exercise the
4 highest degree of care.
5 2. Each motor vehicle operated on the roads and highways of this state shall
6 have present in the vehicle an appropriately endorsed driver who holds a valid driver's
7 license and who maintains the ability to actively control the vehicle at all times.
8 3. No person operating a motor vehicle on the roads and highways of this state shall
9 perform stunt driving, as such term is defined in section 304.145.
10 [3.] 4. Any person who violates the provisions of this section is guilty of a class B
11 misdemeanor, unless an accident is involved then it shall be a class A misdemeanor.
304.975. 1. The provisions of this section shall be known as the "Autonomous
2 Vehicle Public Safety Act".
3 2. For purposes of this section, the following terms mean:
4 (1) "Autonomous technology", technology installed on a motor vehicle that has
5 the capability to drive the motor vehicle without the active control or monitoring of a
6 human operator. The term does not include an active safety system for driver
