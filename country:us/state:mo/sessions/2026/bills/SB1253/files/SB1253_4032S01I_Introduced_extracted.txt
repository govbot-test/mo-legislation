SECOND REGULAR SESSION
SENATE BILL NO. 1253
103RD GENERA L ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY SENATOR HUDSON.
4032S.01I KRISTINA MARTIN, Secretary
AN ACT
To repeal section 575.353, RSMo, and to enact in lieu thereof one new section relating to the
offense of assault on a law enforcement animal, with penalty provisions.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Missouri, as follows:
1 Section A. Section 575.353, RSMo, is repealed and one new
2 section enacted in lieu thereof, to be known as section 575.353,
3 to read as follows:
1 575.353. 1. This section shall be known and may be
2 cited as "Max's Law".
3 2. A person commits the offense of assault on a law
4 enforcement animal if he or she knowingly attempts to kill
5 or disable or knowingly causes or attempts to cause serious
6 physical injury to a law enforcement animal when that animal
7 is involved in law enforcement investigation, apprehension,
8 tracking, or search, or the animal is in the custody of or
9 under the control of a law enforcement officer, department
10 of corrections officer, municipal police department, fire
11 department or a rescue unit or agency.
12 3. The offense of assault on a law enforcement animal
13 is a:
14 (1) Class [A misdemeanor] E felony, if the law
15 enforcement animal is not injured to the point of requiring
16 veterinary care or treatment;
EXPLANATION-Matter enclosed in bold-faced brackets [thus] in this bill is not enacted
and is intended to be omitted in the law.

17 (2) Class [E] D felony if the law enforcement animal
18 is seriously injured to the point of requiring veterinary
