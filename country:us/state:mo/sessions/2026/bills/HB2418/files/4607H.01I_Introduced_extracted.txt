HOUSE BILL NO. 2418
103RD GENERAL ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY REPRESENTATIVE LOY.
4607H.01I JOSEPH ENGLER, Chief Clerk
AN ACT
To amend chapter 595, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to crime victim
assistance.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
Section A. Chapter 595, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto one new section, to be
2 known as section 595.047, to read as follows:
595.047. 1. (1) There is hereby created in the state treasury the "Missouri
2 Crime Victims Fund", which shall consist of moneys appropriated by the general
3 assembly and any gifts, contributions, grants, or other donations from any public or
4 private source. The state treasurer shall be custodian of the fund. In accordance with
5 sections 30.170 and 30.180, the state treasurer may approve disbursements. The fund
6 shall be a dedicated fund and, upon appropriation, moneys in this fund shall be used
7 solely as provided in this section.
8 (2) Notwithstanding the provisions of section 33.080 to the contrary, any moneys
9 remaining in the fund at the end of the biennium shall not revert to the credit of the
10 general revenue fund.
11 (3) The state treasurer shall invest moneys in the fund in the same manner as
12 other funds are invested. Any interest and moneys earned on such investments shall be
13 credited to the fund.
14 2. Moneys in the Missouri crime victims fund shall be disbursed to entitlement
15 jurisdictions, eligible entities, or local governmental entities that are eligible for victim
16 assistance grants under the federal Victims of Crime Act, Pub. L. 98-473, and related
17 regulations, and such jurisdictions and entities shall use such moneys for the same
