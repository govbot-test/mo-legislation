SECOND REGULAR SESSION
103RD GENERAL ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY REPRESENTATIVE VERNETTI.
7296H.01I JOSEPH ENGLER, Chief Clerk
AN ACT
To amend chapter 355, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to nonprofit
corporation hiring practices.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:

Section 2:
Section A. Chapter 355, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto one new section, to be
2 known as section 355.910, to read as follows:

Section 3:
355.910. Any nonprofit corporation that is a nonprofit corporation regulated
2 under Section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code shall, for any employee hired to
3 provide personal services for any political purpose or to take an active part in political
4 management or a political campaign, use the E-Verify system created under Section 403
5 (a) of the Illegal Immigration Reform and Immigrant Responsibility Act of 1996, as
6 amended.
✔
EXPLANATION — Matter enclosed in bold-faced brackets [thus] in the above bill is not enacted and is
intended to be omitted from the law. Matter in bold-face type in the above bill is proposed language.
