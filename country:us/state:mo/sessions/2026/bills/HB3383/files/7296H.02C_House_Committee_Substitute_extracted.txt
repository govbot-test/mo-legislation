Section 1:
SECOND REGULAR SESSION
HOUSE COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE FOR
103RD GENERAL ASSEMBLY
7296H.02C JOSEPH ENGLER, Chief Clerk
AN ACT
To amend chapter 355, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to nonprofit
corporation hiring practices, with penalty provisions.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:

Section 2:
Section A. Chapter 355, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto one new section, to be
2 known as section 355.910, to read as follows:

Section 3:
355.910. 1. The provisions of this section shall apply to:
2 (1) Any nonprofit corporation that is a nonprofit corporation regulated under
3 Section 501(c)(3) or Section 501(c)(4) of the Internal Revenue Code; and
4 (2) Any nonprofit organization organized under the laws of this state.
5 2. An entity listed in subsection 1 of this section shall, for any employee hired to
6 provide personal services for any political purpose or to take an active part in political
7 management or a political campaign, use the E-Verify system created under Section 403
8 (a) of the Illegal Immigration Reform and Immigrant Responsibility Act of 1996, as
9 amended.
10 3. A violation of subsection 2 of this section shall result in the following civil
11 penalties:
12 (1) For a first offense, a fine between two hundred fifty and two thousand dollars
13 per employee hired to provide personal services for any political purpose or to take an
14 active part in political management or a political campaign without the use of the E-
15 Verify system;
16 (2) For a second offense, a fine between two thousand and five thousand dollars
17 per employee hired to provide personal services for any political purpose or to take an
EXPLANATION — Matter enclosed in bold-faced brackets [thus] in the above bill is not enacted and is
intended to be omitted from the law. Matter in bold-face type in the above bill is proposed language.

18 active part in political management or a political campaign without use of the E-Verify
19 system; or
20 (3) For any subsequent offense, a fine between three thousand and ten thousand
21 dollars per day per employee hired to provide personal services for any political purpose
22 or to take an active part in political management or a political campaign without the use
23 of the E-Verify system.
24 4. If an entity listed in subsection 1 of this section uses the E-Verify system
25 created under Section 403(a) of the Illegal Immigration Reform and Immigrant
26 Responsibility Act of 1996, as amended, is notified that the employee is not authorized to
27 work in the United States, and continues to employ such employee, the entity shall be
28 subject to the following civil penalties:
29 (1) For a first offense, a fine between two hundred fifty and two thousand dollars
30 per employee;
31 (2) For a second offense, a fine between two thousand and five thousand dollars
32 per employee; or
33 (3) For any subsequent offense, a fine between three thousand and ten thousand
34 dollars per day per employee.
