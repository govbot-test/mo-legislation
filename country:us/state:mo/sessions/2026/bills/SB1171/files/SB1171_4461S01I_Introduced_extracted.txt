SECOND REGULAR SESSION
SENATE BILL NO. 1171
103RD GENERA L ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY SENATOR WASHINGTON.
4461S.01I KRISTINA MARTIN, Secretary
AN ACT
To amend chapter 571, RSMo, by adding thereto two new sections relating to the offense of
community endangerment due to unsafe storage of a firearm, with penalty provisions.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Missouri, as follows:
1 Section A. Chapter 571, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto
2 two new sections, to be known as sections 571.400 and 571.401,
3 to read as follows:
1 571.400. 1. As used in this section, the following
2 terms mean:
3 (1) "Secure gun storage", a safe, gun safe, gun case,
4 lock box, or other device that is designed to be or can be
5 used to store a firearm and that is designed to be unlocked
6 only by means of a key, a combination, or other similar
7 means;
8 (2) "Unauthorized person", a person who is prohibited
9 from possessing a firearm under state or federal law.
10 2. A person commits the offense of community
11 endangerment due to unsafe storage of a firearm in the first
12 degree if a person stores or leaves a firearm in a location
13 where the person knows or reasonably should know that an
14 unauthorized person may gain access to the firearm and such
15 unauthorized person causes personal injury or death with the
16 firearm.
