AN ACT
To repeal sections 287.200, 287.470, 287.610, 287.615, 287.812, 287.835, and 621.045,
RSMo, and to enact in lieu thereof seven new sections relating to workers'
compensation.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
