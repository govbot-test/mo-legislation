SECOND REGULAR SESSION
HOUSE BILL NO. 2845
103RD GENERAL ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY REPRESENTATIVE DURNELL.
6368H.01I JOSEPH ENGLER, Chief Clerk
AN ACT
To amend chapter 227, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to the designation
of a memorial bridge.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
Section A. Chapter 227, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto one new section, to be
2 known as section 227.905, to read as follows:
227.905. The bridge on Business 63 crossing over U.S. Highway 60 in Howell
2 County shall be designated the "Jerrad Bennett Memorial Bridge". The department of
3 transportation shall erect and maintain appropriate signs designating such bridge, with
4 the costs to be paid by private donations.
