AN ACT
To repeal section 565.056, RSMo, and to enact in lieu thereof seven new sections relating to
workplace violence prevention in health care settings, with penalty provisions.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Missouri, as follows:
1 Section A. Section 565.056, RSMo, is repealed and seven
2 new sections enacted in lieu thereof, to be known as sections
3 191.1903, 191.1906, 191.1909, 191.1912, 191.1915, 197.708, and
