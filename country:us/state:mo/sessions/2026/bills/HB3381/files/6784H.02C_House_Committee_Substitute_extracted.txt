SECOND REGULAR SESSION
HOUSE COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE FOR
HOUSE BILL NO. 3381
103RD GENERAL ASSEMBLY
6784H.02C JOSEPH ENGLER, Chief Clerk
AN ACT
To amend chapters 49, 67, and 82, RSMo, by adding thereto three new sections relating to
penalties on delinquent property taxes.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
Section A. Chapters 49, 67, and 82, RSMo, are amended by adding thereto three new
2 sections, to be known as sections 49.301, 67.740, and 82.1060, to read as follows:
49.301. 1. The commission may, by order or ordinance, prohibit the collection of
2 any late fees or penalties on delinquent property taxes.
3 2. The order or ordinance under subsection 1 of this section may prohibit such
4 collection only in unusual circumstances or circumstances beyond the taxpayer's
5 control.
67.740. 1. The governing body of a constitutional charter county may, by order
2 or ordinance, prohibit the collection of any late fees or penalties on delinquent property
3 taxes.
4 2. The order or ordinance under subsection 1 of this section may prohibit such
5 collection only in unusual circumstances or circumstances beyond the taxpayer's
6 control.
82.1060. 1. The governing body of a city not within a county may, by order or
2 ordinance, prohibit the collection of any late fees or penalties on delinquent property
3 taxes.
4 2. The order or ordinance under subsection 1 of this section may prohibit such
5 collection only in unusual circumstances or circumstances beyond the taxpayer's
6 control.
