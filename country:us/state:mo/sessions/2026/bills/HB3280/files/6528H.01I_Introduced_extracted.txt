Section 1:
SECOND REGULAR SESSION
103RD GENERAL ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY REPRESENTATIVE BOYKIN.
6528H.01I JOSEPH ENGLER, Chief Clerk
AN ACT
To amend chapter 301, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to special license
plates for surviving spouses of disabled veterans.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:

Section 2:
Section A. Chapter 301, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto one new section, to be
2 known as section 301.4102, to read as follows:

Section 3:
301.4102. 1. Any person who is a surviving spouse of person who qualified for
2 disabled veteran license plates under this chapter may apply for "Surviving Spouse"
3 motor vehicle license plates for any motor vehicle such person owns, either solely or
4 jointly, other than an apportioned motor vehicle or a commercial motor vehicle licensed
5 in excess of twenty-four thousand pounds gross weight.
6 2. Any such person shall make application for the surviving spouse license plates
7 on a form provided by the director of revenue and shall furnish such proof of status as a
8 surviving spouse as the director may require. The director shall then issue license plates
9 bearing letters or numbers or a combination thereof as determined by the director with
10 the words "SURVIVING SPOUSE" in place of the words "SHOW-ME STATE". The
11 words "OF DISABLED VETERAN" shall appear on the bottom of such license plates.
12 Such license plates shall be made with fully reflective material with a common color
13 scheme and design, shall be clearly visible at night, and shall be aesthetically attractive,
14 as prescribed by section 301.130.
15 3. There shall be a fifteen dollar fee in addition to the regular registration fees
16 charged for each set of surviving spouse license plates issued pursuant to this section. A
17 fee for the issuance of personalized license plates pursuant to section 301.144 shall not
