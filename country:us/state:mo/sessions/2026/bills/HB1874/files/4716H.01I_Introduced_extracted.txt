SECOND REGULAR SESSION
HOUSE BILL NO. 1874
103RD GENERAL ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY REPRESENTATIVE REUTER.
4716H.01I JOSEPH ENGLER, Chief Clerk
AN ACT
To amend chapter 537, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to a cause of action
based on alienation of affection.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
Section A. Chapter 537, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto one new section, to be
2 known as section 537.552, to read as follows:
537.552. 1. A person may bring a cause of action against a third party for
2 alienation of affection. The third party may be liable for civil damages, to be
3 determined by the court, to the person bringing such action.
4 2. (1) An action for alienation of affection shall be commenced within three
5 years from the last act of the third party giving rise to the cause of action.
6 (2) A person may commence a cause of action for alienation of affection against a
7 natural person only.
