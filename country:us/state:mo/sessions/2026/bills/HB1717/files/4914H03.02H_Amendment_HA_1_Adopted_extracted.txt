7 (1) "Applicant", an entity that applies for a grant under section 191.1616;

12 ibogaine analogs.
13 191.1616. 1. Subject to appropriation, the department shall award grants to conduct
14 certified clinical drug development trials overseen by the United States Food and Drug
15 Administration on the use of ibogaine for the treatment of opioid use disorder, co-occurring
16 substance use disorder, or any other neurological or mental health condition for which ibogaine
17 demonstrates efficacy. The department shall award grants only to an entity that satisfies all of
18 the following:
19 (1) Is located within this state;
20 (2) Has a history of proven research and treatment of neurological diseases and expertise
21 in substance dependence, emotional trauma, and physical or neurological trauma;
22 (3) Has a neurosurgery program with the requisite clinical and research facilities and that
23 is:
24 (a) Staffed by professionals having expertise in the most challenging neurological and
25 neurosurgical conditions; and
26 (b) Capable of providing the necessary infrastructure and expertise to deliver cardiac
27 intensive care services;
28 (4) Has the ability to facilitate pioneering research and innovation in diagnosis and
29 treatment of neurological conditions;
