Section 1:
SECOND REGULAR SESSION
103RD GENERA L ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY SENATOR BRATTIN.
5151S.01I KRISTINA MARTIN, Secretary
AN ACT
To amend chapter 188, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to abortion.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Missouri, as follows:
1 Section A. Chapter 188, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto
2 one new section, to be known as section 188.140, to read as
3 follows:
1 188.140. 1. As used in this section, the term "pro-
2 life individual or organization", shall mean any individual
3 or any nonprofit domestic firm, partnership, association, or
4 corporation, including those which operate under the
5 standards of section 501(c) of the Internal Revenue Code of
6 1986 as amended, where the interests or purposes of such
7 individual or organization include, but shall not be limited
8 to, protecting the sanctity of life or defending the right
9 to life of all humans, born and unborn.
10 2. Any pro-life individual or organization may
11 intervene in any action in which a provision of the
12 Constitution of Missouri, any Missouri statute, or any
13 Missouri rule that involves the regulation of abortion or
14 where the rights of an unborn child is at issue. The first
15 pro-life individual or organization to file for intervention
16 shall represent the rights of the unborn child in such
17 proceeding and shall have the right to file such pleading
18 necessary on behalf of the unborn child. Any other pro-life
SB 1222 2
19 individuals or organizations may submit amicus curiae briefs
20 on their own behalf or on behalf of the unborn child.
