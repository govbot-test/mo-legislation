1 AMEND House Committee Substitute for House Bill Nos. 1838, 1692, 1695, 1983, 2036, 2662
2 & 2743, Page 11, Section 301.190, Lines 116-130, by deleting said lines and inserting in lieu
3 thereof the following:
4
5 "10. (1) When an application is made for an original Missouri certificate of ownership
6 for a motor vehicle previously registered or titled in a state other than Missouri or as required by
7 section 301.020, it shall be accompanied by a current inspection form certified by a duly
8 authorized official inspection station as described in chapter 307, except as provided under
9 subdivision (2) of this subsection. The completed form shall certify that the manufacturer's
10 identification number for the vehicle has been inspected, that it is correctly displayed on the
11 vehicle and shall certify the reading shown on the odometer at the time of inspection. The
12 inspection station shall collect the same fee as authorized in section 307.365 for making the
13 inspection, and the fee shall be deposited in the same manner as provided in section 307.365. If
14 the vehicle is also to be registered in Missouri, [the safety inspection required in chapter 307
15 and] the emissions inspection required under chapter 643 shall be completed and[ only] the fees
16 required by [section 307.365 and] section 643.315 shall be charged to the owner. This section
17 shall not apply to vehicles being transferred on a manufacturer's statement of origin.
18 (2) A licensed new or used motor vehicle dealer who has sold at least two hundred
19 vehicles in the previous calendar year may complete the inspection form required under
20 subdivision (1) of this subsection for any motor vehicle:
21 (a) Having less than thirty thousand miles for the three-year period following the model
22 year of manufacture; or
23 (b) Sold to a person who lives in another state and intends to register the vehicle in
24 another state.
25
26 Any motor vehicle dealer who intentionally completes an inspection form improperly or with
27 incorrect information shall be subject to disciplinary action in accordance with the provisions of
28 this chapter, which may include suspension or revocation of their dealer's license. Any motor
29 vehicle dealer who completes an inspection shall submit the completed form to the department of
30 revenue in a manner prescribed by the department. Such inspections and inspection forms shall
31 be completed on licensed dealership premises."; and
32
33 Further amend said bill, Page 14, Section 307.350, Lines 1-19, by deleting said lines and
34 inserting in lieu thereof the following:
35

37 following:
38
39 "A police officer may issue a citation upon observation of a vehicle on any highway with
40 two or more defective components. The citation shall be reported to the Missouri state highway
41 patrol. The owner of such vehicle shall cause the vehicle to be inspected and obtain a certificate
42 of inspection and approval, sticker, seal, or other device from a duly authorized official
43 inspection station and shall submit such official certificate of inspection and approval, sticker,
Page 2 of 3
4294H02.11H
1 seal, or other device to the Missouri state highway patrol within sixty days of the issuance of the
2 citation. If a certificate of inspection and approval, sticker, seal, or other device is not submitted
3 to the Missouri state highway patrol within sixty days of the issuance of the citation, the
4 registration information of such vehicle shall be reported by the Missouri state highway patrol to
5 the director of revenue and the director shall suspend the registration of such vehicle and not
6 renew the registration until a certificate of inspection and approval, sticker, seal, or other device
7 is obtained.
8 4. For the purposes of this section, the term “components” refers to any safety equipment
9 described in written instructions issued by the superintendent of the Missouri state highway
10 patrol to official inspection stations pursuant to subsection 1 of section 307.360, including, but
11 not limited to, brakes, lighting equipment, signaling devices, steering mechanisms, horns,
12 mirrors, windshield wipers, tires, wheels, exhaust system, glazing, air pollution control devices,
13 and fuel system.
14 5."; and
15
16 Further amend said bill by amending the title, enacting clause, and intersectional references
17 accordingly.
