Section 1:
SECOND REGULAR SESSION
103RD GENERAL ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY REPRESENTATIVE BYRNES.
5382H.01I JOSEPH ENGLER, Chief Clerk
AN ACT
To amend chapter 443, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to loans obtained
with a quitclaim deed.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:

Section 2:
Section A. Chapter 443, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto one new section, to be
2 known as section 443.1060, to read as follows:

Section 3:
443.1060. A person shall not obtain a loan with a quitclaim deed unless the
2 person is a party to the original warranty deed or obtained title to the real property
3 through a quiet title action.
✔
EXPLANATION — Matter enclosed in bold-faced brackets [thus] in the above bill is not enacted and is
intended to be omitted from the law. Matter in bold-face type in the above bill is proposed language.
