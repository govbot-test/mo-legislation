SECOND REGULAR SESSION
HOUSE BILL NO. 3514
103RD GENERAL ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY REPRESENTATIVE CUPPS.
7515H.01I JOSEPH ENGLER, Chief Clerk
AN ACT
To amend chapter 229, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to roundabout
construction.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
Section A. Chapter 229, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto one new section, to be
2 known as section 229.224, to read as follows:
229.224. Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, prior to
2 constructing a roundabout on any federal highway or numbered state highway or state
3 route within any county, city, town, or village, the department of transportation shall
4 obtain the consent of the governing body of such county, city, town, or village. The
5 county, city, town, or village shall report to the department its decision on a request
6 within thirty days of the department's request for consent to begin work on a project.
7 For purposes of this section, "roundabout" means a road junction at which traffic
8 moves in one direction around a central island to reach one of the roads converging in it.
