SECOND REGULAR SESSION
HOUSE BILL NO. 2290
103RD GENERAL ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY REPRESENTATIVE BLACK.
5963H.01I JOSEPH ENGLER, Chief Clerk
AN ACT
To amend chapter 10, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to the official state
cherry blossom festival.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
Section A. Chapter 10, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto one new section, to be
2 known as section 10.284, to read as follows:
10.284. The Missouri Cherry Blossom Festival in the city of Marshfield is
2 selected for and shall be known as the official state cherry blossom festival for the state
3 of Missouri.
✔
EXPLANATION — Matter enclosed in bold-faced brackets [thus] in the above bill is not enacted and is
intended to be omitted from the law. Matter in bold-face type in the above bill is proposed language.
