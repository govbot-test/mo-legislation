AN ACT
To repeal section 208.159, RSMo, and to enact in lieu thereof one new section relating to MO
HealthNet payments for nursing home services.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
Section A. Section 208.159, RSMo, is repealed and one new section enacted in lieu
2 thereof, to be known as section 208.159, to read as follows:
208.159. 1. Notwithstanding the provisions of sections 207.010, 208.152, and
2 208.153, the department of social services shall administer payments for nursing home
3 services authorized in sections 208.151, et seq., which govern medical assistance under Title
4 XIX, Public Law 89-97, 1965 amendments to the Federal Social Security Act (42 U.S.C. 301
5 et seq.), as amended, and shall administer vendor payments for the aged and direct adult
6 services for the aged under Title XX, Public Law 93-647, 1974 amendments to the Federal
7 Social Security Act (42 U.S.C. 1397 et seq.), as amended. The department shall, pursuant to
8 chapter 536, promulgate rules and regulations for the purpose of administering such
9 payments, including rules to define the reasonable costs, manner, extent, quality, charges and
10 fees or payments for nursing home services.
11 2. The department of social services shall apply to the Centers for Medicare and

13 waiver or state plan amendment to implement a patient-driven payment model for MO
14 HealthNet payments for nursing home services provided by facilities that:
15 (1) Calculates payments for the following patient care services based on patients'
16 clinical characteristics rather than the volume of services provided:
17 (a) Physical therapy;
