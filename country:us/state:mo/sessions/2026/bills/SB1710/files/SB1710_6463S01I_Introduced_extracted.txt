AN ACT
To amend chapters 160 and 161, RSMo, by adding thereto two new sections relating to support for
high school students' sleep requirements.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Missouri, as follows:
1 Section A. Chapters 160 and 161, RSMo, are amended by
2 adding thereto two new sections, to be known as sections 160.044
3 and 161.044, to read as follows:
1 160.044. 1. No high school shall begin mandated
2 instruction for students earlier than 8:30 a.m. This
3 requirement shall be in effect at all school districts
4 statewide beginning with the earliest of:
5 (1) The commencement of the 2027-28 school year; or
6 (2) The expiration of the school district's existing
7 collective bargaining agreement establishing school day
8 start times.
9 2. A school district facing unique challenges as a
10 result of the requirements of subsection 1 of this section
11 may apply to the department of elementary and secondary
12 education for a waiver from such requirements. The
13 department shall establish a form that districts may use for
14 this purpose. To be eligible for a waiver, a school
15 district shall supply substantial evidence of significant
16 hardship due to factors including, but not limited to,
17 operations, funding, or pupil transportation.
