AN ACT
To amend chapter 442, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to homeowners'
associations.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
Section A. Chapter 442, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto one new section, to be
2 known as section 442.405, to read as follows:
442.405. 1. As used in this section, "homeowners' association" or "association"
2 shall mean a nonprofit corporation or unincorporated association of homeowners
3 created under a declaration to own and operate portions of a planned community or
4 other residential subdivision that has the power under the declaration to assess
5 association members to pay the costs and expenses incurred in the performance of the
6 association's obligations under the declaration or tenants-in-common with respect to the
7 ownership of common ground or amenities of a planned community or other residential
8 subdivision. This term shall not include a condominium unit owners' association as
9 defined and provided for in subdivision (3) of section 448.1-103 or a residential
10 cooperative.
11 2. (1) Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, a homeowners'
12 association shall dissolve ten years following the date of establishment unless such
13 association is renewed by a majority vote of eighty-five percent of all members of the
14 association at an election held within ninety days of the date of dissolution. Any
15 association failing to receive a majority vote of eighty-five percent for renewal shall be
16 immediately dissolved, and the member homeowners shall not be subject to any other
