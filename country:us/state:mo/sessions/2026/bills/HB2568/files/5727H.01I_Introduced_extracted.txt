AN ACT
To repeal section 144.014, RSMo, and to enact in lieu thereof one new section relating to food
sales and use tax.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
Section A. Section 144.014, RSMo, is repealed and one new section enacted in lieu
2 thereof, to be known as section 144.014, to read as follows:
144.014. 1. Notwithstanding other provisions of law to the contrary, beginning
2 October 1, 1997, except as provided under subsection 2 of this section, the tax levied and
3 imposed under this chapter on all retail sales of food shall be at the rate of one percent. The
4 revenue derived from the one percent rate pursuant to this section shall be deposited by the
5 state treasurer in the school district trust fund and shall be distributed as provided in section
6 144.701.
7 2. (1) Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, beginning January
8 1, 2027, no state sales or use tax shall be levied or imposed on any retail sale of food in
9 this state. The exemption granted under this subsection shall not apply to local sales tax
10 as defined under section 32.085, or local use tax levied or imposed as authorized by law.
11 If the exemption provided under this subdivision is sunset as provided under
12 subdivision (2) of this subsection, the provisions of the one percent tax imposed on all
13 retail sales of food under subsection 1 of this section shall apply beginning on and after
14 the date of the expiration of the exemption.
15 (2) Under section 23.253 of the Missouri sunset act:
