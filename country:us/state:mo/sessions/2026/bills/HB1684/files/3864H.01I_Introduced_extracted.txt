SECOND REGULAR SESSION
103RD GENERAL ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY REPRESENTATIVE WHALEY.
3864H.01I JOSEPH ENGLER, Chief Clerk
AN ACT
To amend chapter 195, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to over-the-counter
purchase of ivermectin tablets.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:

Section 2:
Section A. Chapter 195, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto one new section, to be
2 known as section 195.1000, to read as follows:

Section 3:
195.1000. Notwithstanding any other provision of law, ivermectin tablets shall be
2 available to the public through over-the-counter purchase in this state without a
3 prescription or consultation with a pharmacist or other health care professional.
✔
EXPLANATION — Matter enclosed in bold-faced brackets [thus] in the above bill is not enacted and is
intended to be omitted from the law. Matter in bold-face type in the above bill is proposed language.
