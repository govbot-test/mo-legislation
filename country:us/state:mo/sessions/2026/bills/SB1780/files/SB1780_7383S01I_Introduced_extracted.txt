Section 1:
SECOND REGULAR SESSION
103RD GENERA L ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY SENATOR BURGER.
7383S.01I KRISTINA MARTIN, Secretary
AN ACT
To amend chapter 407, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to the sale of wood
products.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Missouri, as follows:
1 Section A. Chapter 407, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto
2 one new section, to be known as section 407.3300, to read as
3 follows:
1 407.3300. 1. No person, company, or business shall
2 sell or market products using the term wood without the
3 product's decorative surface being made of solid wood or
4 wood veneer, originating from a tree. No person, company,
5 or business shall advertise or sell any part of a product
6 marketed using wood-related terms or names of wood species
7 including, but not limited to, oak, maple, cherry, pine, and
8 walnut if the product's decorative surface is not that wood
9 species.
10 2. A person, company, or business may sell any product
11 physically located within the state as of August 28, 2026,
12 that is packaged in violation of the provisions of
13 subsection 1 of this section if the product is clearly
14 labeled: "This product is not made from wood.". After
15 August 28, 2026, no additional products shall enter the
16 state if the packaging is in violation of subsection 1 of
17 this section.
SB 1780 2
18 3. The department of agriculture may promulgate all
19 necessary rules and regulations for the administration of
20 this section. Any rule or portion of a rule, as that term
21 is defined in section 536.010, that is created under the
22 authority delegated in this section shall become effective
23 only if it complies with and is subject to all of the
24 provisions of chapter 536 and, if applicable, section
25 536.028. This section and chapter 536 are nonseverable and
26 if any of the powers vested with the general assembly
27 pursuant to chapter 536 to review, to delay the effective
28 date, or to disapprove and annul a rule are subsequently
29 held unconstitutional, then the grant of rulemaking
30 authority and any rule proposed or adopted after August 28,
31 2026, shall be invalid and void.
