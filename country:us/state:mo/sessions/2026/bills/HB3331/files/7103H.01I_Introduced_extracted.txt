SECOND REGULAR SESSION
HOUSE BILL NO. 3331
103RD GENERAL ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY REPRESENTATIVE RIGGS.
7103H.01I JOSEPH ENGLER, Chief Clerk
AN ACT
To amend chapter 21, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to materials
provided to certain committees of the general assembly.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
Section A. Chapter 21, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto one new section, to be
2 known as section 21.1060, to read as follows:
21.1060. Each state department shall, upon request, provide to the budget
2 committee of the house of representatives and the appropriations committee of the
3 senate the following:
4 (1) The total number and names of nonprofit corporations receiving funding
5 from the department;
6 (2) Any copies of Internal Revenue Service Form 990 for such nonprofit
7 corporations in possession of the department;
8 (3) The total number of individuals served by such nonprofit corporation each
9 year, including the zip codes of those served; and
10 (4) Copies of any memorandum of understanding between the department and a
11 nonprofit corporation.
