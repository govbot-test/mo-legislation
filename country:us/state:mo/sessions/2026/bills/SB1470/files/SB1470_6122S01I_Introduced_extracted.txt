1 3.142. 1. There is hereby established in the state
2 treasury a revolving fund known as the "Statutory Revision
3 Fund", and which shall receive funds paid to the revisor of
4 statutes for sales of the revised statutes of Missouri or
5 any supplement thereto, whether in printed, electronic,
6 magnetic, or other form and funds received for any other

3 (1) Provide a research and reference service on
4 legislative problems;
5 (2) Upon written request, make investigation into

16 and render any other service in connection therewith for any
17 member of the general assembly.

2 state convenes until it is adjourned finally, the committee

12 bills, resolutions and measures, [draft them in proper form]

14 matters which fall within the scope of the duties of the
15 committee.

2 meet within [ten] thirty days after the convening of each

17 the general assembly. The regular meeting place of the
