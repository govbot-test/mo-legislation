Section 1:
SECOND REGULAR SESSION
103RD GENERAL ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY REPRESENTATIVE BOYKO.
5884H.02I JOSEPH ENGLER, Chief Clerk
AN ACT
To amend chapter 9, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to racquetball day.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:

Section 2:
Section A. Chapter 9, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto one new section, to be
2 known as section 9.504, to read as follows:

Section 3:
9.504. April fifth each year is hereby designated as "Racquetball Day" in
2 Missouri, in honor of Kirkwood Racquetball, USA Racquetball's four-time consecutive
3 high school national champions. The citizens of this state are encouraged to participate
4 in appropriate events and activities to learn more about the sport of racquetball and
5 celebrate the successes of Kirkwood Racquetball.
✔
EXPLANATION — Matter enclosed in bold-faced brackets [thus] in the above bill is not enacted and is
intended to be omitted from the law. Matter in bold-face type in the above bill is proposed language.
