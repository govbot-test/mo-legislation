AN ACT
To amend chapter 407, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to the sale of wood
products.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
Section A. Chapter 407, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto one new section, to be
2 known as section 407.3300, to read as follows:
407.3300. 1. No person, company, or business shall sell or market products using
2 the term wood without the product's decorative surface being made of solid wood or
3 wood veneer, originating from a tree. No person, company, or business shall advertise
4 or sell any part of a product marketed using wood-related terms or names of wood
5 species including, but not limited to, oak, maple, cherry, pine, and walnut if the
6 product's decorative surface is not that wood species.
7 2. A person, company, or business may sell any product physically located within
8 the state as of August 28, 2026, that is packaged in violation of the provisions of
9 subsection 1 of this section if the product is clearly labeled: "This product is not made
10 from wood.". After August 28, 2026, no additional products shall enter the state if the
11 packaging is in violation of subsection 1 of this section.
12 3. The department of agriculture may promulgate all necessary rules and
13 regulations for the administration of this section. Any rule or portion of a rule, as that
14 term is defined in section 536.010, that is created under the authority delegated in this
15 section shall become effective only if it complies with and is subject to all of the
16 provisions of chapter 536 and, if applicable, section 536.028. This section and chapter
17 536 are nonseverable and if any of the powers vested with the general assembly
