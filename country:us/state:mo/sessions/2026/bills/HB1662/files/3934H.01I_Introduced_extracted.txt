Section 1:
SECOND REGULAR SESSION
103RD GENERAL ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY REPRESENTATIVE SEITZ.
3934H.01I JOSEPH ENGLER, Chief Clerk
AN ACT
To amend chapter 191, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to free breakfast
and lunch in schools.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:

Section 2:
Section A. Chapter 191, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto one new section, to be
2 known as section 191.824, to read as follows:

Section 3:
191.824. For the 2026-27 school year and all subsequent school years, each
2 public school, as such term is defined in section 160.011, shall provide each student
3 enrolled in such public school with one free breakfast and one free lunch each day such
4 student attends classes in such public school.
✔
EXPLANATION — Matter enclosed in bold-faced brackets [thus] in the above bill is not enacted and is
intended to be omitted from the law. Matter in bold-face type in the above bill is proposed language.
