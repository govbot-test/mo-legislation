Section 1:
SECOND REGULAR SESSION
103RD GENERA L ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY SENATOR MAY.
4889S.01I KRISTINA MARTIN, Secretary
AN ACT
To repeal section 57.540, RSMo, and to enact in lieu thereof one new section relating to
compensation for the attorney of the sheriff of the city of St. Louis.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Missouri, as follows:
1 Section A. Section 57.540, RSMo, is repealed and one new
2 section enacted in lieu thereof, to be known as section 57.540,
3 to read as follows:
1 57.540. The sheriff of the City of St. Louis may
2 employ an attorney at law to aid and advise him in the
3 discharge of his duties and to represent him in court, which
4 said attorney shall be known as "sheriff's attorney", and
5 who shall receive as compensation for his services as such
6 sheriff's attorney a sum of not less than [three thousand
7 dollars and not more than] fifteen thousand dollars and not
8 more than seventy-five thousand dollars per annum, payable
9 in semimonthly installments out of the same funds and
10 revenue as the sheriff of such city is paid.
✓
EXPLANATION-Matter enclosed in bold-faced brackets [thus] in this bill is not enacted
and is intended to be omitted in the law.
