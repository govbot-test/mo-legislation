Section 1:
2008H03.03H
HB 2008
Offered By
Marty Joe Murray 78
1 AMEND House Committee Substitute for House Bill No. 2008, Page 6, Section 8.065, Line 22
2 by inserting immediate after said section and line the following:
3
4 "Section 8.066. To the Department of Public Safety
5 For the Office of the Director
6 For distribution to a nonprofit organization that is recognized as a 501(c)(3) tax-exempt entity
7 under the Internal Revenue Code; has been continuously operating in a city not within a county
8 since at least 2018; provides safe and sober housing for individuals managing substance use
9 disorders and co-occurring mental health conditions; operates multiple recovery homes; offers a
10 peer respite program designed for individuals in the early stages of recovery, involving a 30-day
11 intensive therapeutic stay with daily group therapy sessions; provides wrap-around services such
12 as counseling, 24-hour peer support, outpatient programming, and access to Medication-Assisted
13 Treatment (MAT); assists residents with life skills training, job placement, workforce
14 development, and peer pre-apprenticeship programs; and organizes community outreach events
15 and in-house recovery activities, including house meetings and recovery speakers
16 From Temporary Assistance for Needy Families Federal Fund (1199) (one-time). . . . . . .
17 $100,000";
18
19 Further amend said bill by adjusting section and bill totals and intersectional references
20 accordingly.
