SECOND REGULAR SESSION
SENATE BILL NO. 925
103RD GENERA L ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY SENATOR NURRENBERN.
3902S.02I KRISTINA MARTIN, Secretary
AN ACT
To repeal section 41.430, RSMo, and to enact in lieu thereof one new section relating to the
National Guard.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Missouri, as follows:
1 Section A. Section 41.430, RSMo, is repealed and one new
2 section enacted in lieu thereof, to be known as section 41.430,
3 to read as follows:
1 41.430. 1. This section shall be known and may be
2 cited as "The Supporting Missouri Servicemen and Women Act".
3 2. Officers, warrant officers and enlisted personnel
4 of the organized militia on active duty in the service of
5 the state shall receive as compensation the same pay,
6 longevity, and allowances as are or may be provided for
7 members of like grade and branch of service in the Armed
8 Forces of the United States[.], except:
9 (1) Members of the organized militia serving on active
10 duty shall receive as a minimum the daily rate equivalent to
11 the grade level of E5 with maximum longevity and with
12 dependents;
13 (2) If the active duty is less than thirty days, the
14 pay shall be the same as members of like grade and branch of
15 service in the Armed Forces of the United States on active
16 duty for more than thirty days; and
EXPLANATION-Matter enclosed in bold-faced brackets [thus] in this bill is not enacted
and is intended to be omitted in the law.
