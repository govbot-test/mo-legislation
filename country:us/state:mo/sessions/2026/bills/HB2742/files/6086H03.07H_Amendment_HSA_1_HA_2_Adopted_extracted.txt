6086H03.07H
HB 2742
HOUSE SUBSTITUTE AMENDMENT NO. __________
for
Offered By
_____________________________________ ___________________________________
1 AMEND House Committee Substitute for House Bill No. 2742, Page 1, Section 160.3300, Line
2 1, by inserting after the number "160.3300." the number "1."; and
3
4 Further amend said bill, page, and section, Line 9, by inserting after all of said line the
5 following:
6
7 "2. No image or video captured by a school bus safety camera authorized under this
8 section shall be used by a political subdivision for violation detection or enforcement as part of
9 any automated camera system designed to detect traffic violations and issue citations. Nothing
10 in this section shall prohibit a prosecutor from introducing any image or video captured by a
11 school bus safety camera as evidence in a judicial proceeding."; and
12
13 Further amend said bill, Page 5, Section 304.070, Line 7, by inserting after the word "the" the
14 word "physical"; and
15
16 Further amend said bill, page, and section, Line 9, by deleting all of said line and inserting in lieu
17 thereof the following:
18
19 "such violation causes the serious physical injury or death of any child shall be guilty of a class
20 D felony. For the purposes of this subsection, “physical injury" means physical pain, illness, or
21 any impairment of physical condition including, but not limited to, bruising, lacerations,
22 hematomas, welts, or permanent or temporary disfigurement and impairment of any bodily
23 function or organ. The term "serious physical injury" means a physical injury that creates a
24 substantial risk of death or that causes serious disfigurement or protracted loss or impairment of
25 the function of any part of the body."; and
26
27 Further amend said bill by amending the title, enacting clause, and intersectional references
28 accordingly.
29
30 THIS AMENDMENT SUBSTITUTES AMENDMENT 6086H03.02H
