AN ACT
To repeal sections 313.005, 313.055, and 313.057, RSMo, and to enact in lieu thereof two
new sections relating to the sale of bingo cards.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
Section A. Sections 313.005, 313.055, and 313.057, RSMo, are repealed and two new
2 sections enacted in lieu thereof, to be known as sections 313.005 and 313.057, to read as
3 follows:
313.005. As used in sections 313.005 to 313.080, the following terms shall mean:
2 (1) "Bingo", a game in which each participant receives one or more cards, including,
3 but not limited to, pull-tab cards, marked off into twenty-five squares arranged on five
4 horizontal rows of five squares each; or, one or more cards marked off into twenty-five
5 squares arranged on five horizontal rows of five squares each which are not pull-tab cards
6 and, in addition thereto, one or more pull-tab cards. Each square is designated by number,
7 letter or by a combination of numbers and letters, except that the center square on the card
8 shall be designated with the word "free". No two cards shall be identical. As the announcer
9 of the game announces a number, letter or a combination of numbers and letters, each player
10 covers the square corresponding to the announced number, letter or combination by marking
11 such card in ink. The numbers, letters or combination of numbers and letters which are
12 announced shall appear on an object selected by chance, either manually or mechanically,
13 from a receptacle containing the objects bearing numbers, letters or combinations of numbers
14 and letters. The winner of each game shall be the player or players who are first to properly
15 cover a predetermined and announced pattern of squares upon the card or cards used by such
16 player or players. A prize or prizes may be awarded to the winner or winners of a game;
