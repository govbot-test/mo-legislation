Section 1:
SECOND REGULAR SESSION
103RD GENERA L ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY SENATOR BRATTIN.
5191S.01I KRISTINA MARTIN, Secretary
AN ACT
To repeal section 8.016, RSMo, and to enact in lieu thereof one new section relating to security of
the state capitol.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Missouri, as follows:
1 Section A. Section 8.016, RSMo, is repealed and one new
2 section enacted in lieu thereof, to be known as section 8.016,
3 to read as follows:
1 8.016. 1. The commissioner of the office of
2 administration shall provide each member of the senate and
3 each member of the house of representatives with a key that
4 accesses the dome of the state capitol.
5 2. The commissioner of the office of administration
6 shall provide each member of the senate and each member of
7 the house of representatives with a key or key card that
8 accesses the state capitol. Upon written request by a
9 member of the senate or house of representatives, the
10 commissioner of administration shall also provide the
11 member's spouse or children with a key or key card that
12 accesses the state capitol.
13 3. The president pro tem of the senate and the speaker
14 of the house of representatives shall be responsible for
15 providing a training program for the members and staff of
16 the general assembly regarding access to secured areas of
17 the capitol building. They may consult with the office of
SB 1403 2
18 administration and department of public safety when
19 developing such program.
✓
