SECOND REGULAR SESSION
HOUSE BILL NO. 3266
103RD GENERAL ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY REPRESENTATIVE JOHNSON.
7002H.01I JOSEPH ENGLER, Chief Clerk
AN ACT
To amend chapter 213, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to rights of the
unhoused.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
Section A. Chapter 213, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto one new section, to be
2 known as section 213.145, to read as follows:
213.145. No person's rights, privileges, or access to public services shall be
2 denied or abridged solely because such person is experiencing homelessness. Such
3 person shall be granted the same rights and privileges as any other citizen of this state.
4 A person experiencing homelessness has the following rights:
5 (1) The right to move freely in public spaces, including sidewalks and public
6 buildings, parks, and transportation;
7 (2) The right to equal treatment by city and state agencies;
8 (3) The right to emergency medical care;
9 (4) The right to a reasonable expectation of privacy for personal property, just as
10 inside a home;
11 (5) The right to vote, register to vote, and receive documentation necessary to
12 prove identity; and
13 (6) The right to protection from disclosure of his or her records and information
14 without appropriate legal authority and the right to confidentiality of personal records.
