To repeal section 161.412, RSMo, and to enact in lieu thereof one new section relating to
grants to deaf-blind individuals and families.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
Section A. Section 161.412, RSMo, is repealed and one new section enacted in lieu

161.412. 1. Subject to appropriations, the Missouri commission for the deaf and hard
