SECOND REGULAR SESSION
HOUSE COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE FOR
HOUSE BILL NO. 3006
103RD GENERAL ASSEMBLY
5527H.04C JOSEPH ENGLER, Chief Clerk
AN ACT
To amend chapter 30, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to the state treasurer.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
Section A. Chapter 30, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto one new section, to be
2 known as section 30.980, to read as follows:
30.980. 1. There is hereby created within the office of the state treasurer the
2 position of "Coordinator of Federal Funds Recovery". The coordinator's primary duty
3 shall be monitoring the status of all federal funds appropriated to the state of Missouri.
4 2. The coordinator shall be authorized to attempt to recover any funds
5 appropriated but not disbursed to the state of Missouri through any available means
6 including, but not limited to, filing actions in a court of competent jurisdiction.
7 3. The coordinator shall establish a process for state employees to report funds
8 that were appropriated by the federal government but never disbursed. The
9 coordinator shall not be required to recover federal funds that simply pass through
10 the state on their way to local allocations in the form of grants, formula shares, or
11 federal supplementals. However, the coordinator shall ensure the state does exact its
12 administrative share from federal pass-through funds.
