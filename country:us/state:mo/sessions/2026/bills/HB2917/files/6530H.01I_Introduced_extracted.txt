To amend chapter 192, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to the senior tenant
bill of rights.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:

Section 2:

2 known as section 192.2160, to read as follows:

Section 3:

2 individuals sixty years of age or older.

4 following:
5 (1) Provide written lease agreements to older adults before they take possession
6 of the property;
7 (2) Provide written lease agreement renewals at least thirty days prior to the end
8 of the previous lease;
9 (3) Honor all lease terms agreed upon between the landlord and older adult
10 tenant;
