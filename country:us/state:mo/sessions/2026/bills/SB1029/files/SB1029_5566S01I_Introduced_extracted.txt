Section 1:
SECOND REGULAR SESSION
103RD GENERA L ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY SENATOR BRATTIN.
5566S.01I KRISTINA MARTIN, Secretary
AN ACT
To amend chapter 162, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to education.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Missouri, as follows:
1 Section A. Chapter 162, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto
2 one new section, to be known as section 162.192, to read as
3 follows:
1 162.192. Notwithstanding any provision of law to the
2 contrary, each school district shall maintain, on its
3 publicly accessible website, a readily identifiable and
4 easily accessible ledger setting forth all financial
5 transactions conducted with school district funds,
6 including, but not limited to, all income and all
7 expenditures. Each public school shall post, in a
8 conspicuous and prominently displayed location on its own
9 website, a direct link to the school district's financial
10 ledger.
✓
