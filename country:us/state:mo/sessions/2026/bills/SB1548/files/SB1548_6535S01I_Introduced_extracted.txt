Section 1:
SECOND REGULAR SESSION
103RD GENERA L ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY SENATOR COLEMAN.
6535S.01I KRISTINA MARTIN, Secretary
AN ACT
To amend chapter 640, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to discharging fluoride
into water used for human consumption.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Missouri, as follows:
1 Section A. Chapter 640, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto
2 one new section, to be known as section 640.1000, to read as
3 follows:
1 640.1000. 1. Notwithstanding any provision of law to
2 the contrary, it shall be unlawful for a public water supply
3 district, operating under chapter 247, or a public water
4 system, as defined in section 640.102, to discharge fluoride
5 into water used for human consumption.
6 2. Any individual may report violations of this
7 section to the department of natural resources. If the
8 department finds that a violation occurred, the department
9 shall ask the attorney general to commence a civil action in
10 a court of competent jurisdiction. If the court finds that
11 a violation occurred, the court may grant damages,
12 injunctive relief, attorney fees, and any such other relief
13 the court finds appropriate.
