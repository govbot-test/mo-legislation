AN ACT
To amend chapter 376, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to notification of
out-of-network status by health care providers.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
Section A. Chapter 376, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto one new section, to be
2 known as section 376.683, to read as follows:
376.683. 1. As used in this section, the following terms mean:
2 (1) "Emergency medical condition", the same meaning given to the term in
3 section 376.1350;
4 (2) "Health benefit plan", the same meaning given to the term in section
5 376.1350;
6 (3) "Health care provider", the same meaning given to the term in section
7 376.1350;
8 (4) "Health care service", the same meaning given to the term in section
9 376.1350;
10 (5) "Nonemergency care", any health care service for a medical condition that is
11 not an emergency medical condition.
12 2. Before a health care provider provides any nonemergency care for a patient,
13 the health care provider shall:
14 (1) Determine whether the patient is covered under a health benefit plan;
15 (2) Determine its network status with any health benefit plan under which the
16 patient is covered; and
