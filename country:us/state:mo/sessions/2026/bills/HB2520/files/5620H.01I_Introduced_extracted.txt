HOUSE BILL NO. 2520
103RD GENERAL ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY REPRESENTATIVE REED.
5620H.01I JOSEPH ENGLER, Chief Clerk
AN ACT
To amend chapter 1, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to federal
Immigration and Customs Enforcement operations, with a penalty provision.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
Section A. Chapter 1, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto one new section, to be
2 known as section 1.3060, to read as follows:
1.3060. 1. Notwithstanding any other provision of law, a federal law
2 enforcement agent performing Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE)
3 operations in this state shall not conceal his or her identity as an ICE agent or wear
4 a facial covering that conceals or obscures his or her facial identity in the performance
5 of his or her duties, except as expressly authorized in this section.
6 2. For purposes of this section, "facial covering" means any opaque mask,
7 garment, helmet, headgear, or other item that conceals or obscures the facial identity of
8 an individual including, but not limited to, a balaclava, tactical mask, gaiter, ski mask,
9 and any similar type of facial covering or face-shielding item. A facial covering does not
10 include any of the following:
11 (1) A translucent face shield or clear mask that does not conceal the wearer's
12 facial identity;
13 (2) An N95 medical mask or surgical mask to protect against transmission of
14 disease or infection, or any other mask, helmet, or device including, but not limited to,
15 air-purifying respirators, full or half masks, or self-contained breathing apparatus
16 necessary to protect against exposure to any toxin, gas, smoke, inclement weather, or
17 any other hazardous or harmful environmental condition;
