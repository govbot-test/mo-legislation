103RD GENERAL ASSEMBLY

4186H.01I JOSEPH ENGLER, Chief Clerk
AN ACT
To amend chapter 650, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to the
establishment of an alert system for missing persons with developmental disabilities.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
Section A. Chapter 650, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto one new section, to be
2 known as section 650.535, to read as follows:
650.535. 1. There is hereby created within the department of public safety a
2 statewide program called the "Purple Alert System" to aid in the identification and
3 location of missing persons with developmental disabilities whose disappearance poses a
4 credible threat of immediate danger or serious bodily harm to themselves through
5 immediate and effective community and emergency response.
6 2. For purposes of this section, "developmental disability" includes a mental or
7 cognitive disability that is not Alzheimer's disease or a dementia-related disorder; an
8 intellectual disability; a brain injury; or other physical, mental, or emotional disabilities
9 that are not related to substance abuse; or any combination of such conditions.
10 3. Before July 1, 2027, the department of public safety, in cooperation with the
11 state highway patrol, department of transportation, department of health and senior
12 services, the state lottery, and local law enforcement agencies shall:
13 (1) Develop a training program and alert system for missing persons who have
14 developmental disabilities that is compatible with existing alert systems in the state. The
15 training program shall implement crisis intervention team training to equip law
16 enforcement officers with the skills necessary to understand developmental disabilities,
17 to de-escalate interactions with persons in crisis, to facilitate appropriate interventions,

20 (2) Establish policies and procedures for responding to a reported missing

33 (b) Inform all on-duty law enforcement officers of the reported missing person

35 (c) Communicate the report to all other law enforcement agencies in the counties

37 4. The department of public safety shall administer and promulgate rules for the
38 implementation of the alert system established under this section. Any rule or portion
39 of a rule, as that term is defined in section 536.010, that is created under the authority

41 all of the provisions of chapter 536 and, if applicable, section 536.028. This section and
42 chapter 536 are nonseverable and if any of the powers vested with the general assembly
43 pursuant to chapter 536 to review, to delay the effective date, or to disapprove and annul
