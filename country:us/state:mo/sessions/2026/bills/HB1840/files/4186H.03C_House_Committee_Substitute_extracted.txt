103RD GENERAL ASSEMBLY

AN ACT
To amend chapter 650, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to the
establishment of an alert system for missing persons with developmental
disabilities, with an emergency clause.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
Section A. Chapter 650, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto one new section, to be
2 known as section 650.535, to read as follows:
650.535. 1. There is hereby created within the department of public safety a
2 statewide program called the "Purple Alert System" to aid in the identification and
3 location of missing persons with developmental disabilities whose disappearance poses a
4 credible threat of immediate danger or serious bodily harm to themselves through
5 immediate and effective community and emergency response.
6 2. For purposes of this section, "developmental disability" includes the
7 following:
8 (1) A mental or cognitive disability, which shall not include, for persons sixty
9 years of age or older, Alzheimer's disease or a dementia-related disorder;
10 (2) An intellectual disability;
11 (3) A brain injury;
12 (4) Other physical, mental, or emotional disabilities that are not related to
13 substance abuse; or
14 (5) Any combination of such conditions.
15 3. Before July 1, 2027, the department of public safety, in cooperation with the
16 Missouri state highway patrol, department of transportation, department of health and
17 senior services, the state lottery, and local law enforcement agencies shall:
