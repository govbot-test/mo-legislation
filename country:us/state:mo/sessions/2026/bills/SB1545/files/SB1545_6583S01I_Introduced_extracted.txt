Section 1:
SECOND REGULAR SESSION
103RD GENERA L ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY SENATOR BEAN.
6583S.01I KRISTINA MARTIN, Secretary
AN ACT
To repeal section 313.660, RSMo, relating to off-track pari-mutuel wagering on horse racing.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Missouri, as follows:
1 Section A. Section 313.660, RSMo, is repealed, to read as
2 follows:
1 [313.660. 1. No individual shall for a
2 fee, directly or indirectly, accept anything of
3 value to be wagered or to be transmitted or
4 delivered for wager in any pari-mutuel system of
5 wagering on horse racing or for a fee deliver
6 anything of value which has been received
7 outside of the enclosure of a race track holding
8 a horse race licensed under sections 313.500 to
9 313.710 to be placed as wagers in the pari-
10 mutuel pool within such enclosure.
11 2. Any individual violating the provisions
12 of this section shall upon conviction be guilty
13 of a class D felony.]
✓
EXPLANATION-Matter enclosed in bold-faced brackets [thus] in this bill is not enacted
and is intended to be omitted in the law.
