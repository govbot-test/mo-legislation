WHEREAS, the Women Legislators of Missouri Caucus sponsors the annual
2 DeVerne Lee Calloway Award, in honor of the first African-American member of the
3 Missouri General Assembly, to recognize outstanding female leaders in Missouri; and
4
5 WHEREAS, the Women Legislators of Missouri Caucus sponsors the Women
6 Legislators of Missouri Scholarship to recognize exceptional high school seniors on the basis
7 of leadership, academics, and community service; and

12 and to recognize the young women who are recipients of the 2026 Women Legislators of
13 Missouri Scholarship award:
14

16 House of Representatives, One Hundred Third General Assembly, grant the Women
17 Legislators of Missouri Caucus use of the House Chamber on Monday, April 13, 2026, from
18 2:00 p.m. to 3:00 p.m. to recognize the 2026 DeVerne Lee Calloway Award recipient and the
19 2026 scholarship recipients.

21 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the sponsor of this House Resolution,
22 Representative Pattie Mansur, or her designee, shall be present in the House Chamber and
23 shall assume responsibility for the group's actions for the duration of the aforementioned
24 event.
