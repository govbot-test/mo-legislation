SECOND REGULAR SESSION
HOUSE BILL NO. 2421
103RD GENERAL ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY REPRESENTATIVE HALEY.
5644H.01I JOSEPH ENGLER, Chief Clerk
AN ACT
To amend chapter 644, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to water storage in
a reservoir owned by the U.S. Army Corps of Engineers.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
Section A. Chapter 644, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto one new section, to be
2 known as section 644.083, to read as follows:
644.083. 1. Notwithstanding any other provision of law, a person who has
2 contracted for the right to store water in a reservoir owned by the U.S. Army Corps of
3 Engineers shall have exclusive rights to any return flows generated directly or indirectly
4 to that reservoir by the person. The rights conferred by this section shall be subject to
5 any regulatory requirements imposed by this state and to the availability to the person
6 of unused storage capacity within the reservoir to store such return flows.
7 2. As used in this section, "return flow" means water that is discharged directly
8 or indirectly to a reservoir from a water reclamation facility.
