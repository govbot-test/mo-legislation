3832S.01P KRISTINA MARTIN, Secretary
AN ACT
To repeal section 162.481, RSMo, and to enact in lieu thereof two new sections relating to urban
school districts.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Missouri, as follows:
1 Section A. Section 162.481, RSMo, is repealed and two new
2 sections enacted in lieu thereof, to be known as sections
3 162.481 and 167.1001, to read as follows:
1 162.481. 1. Except as otherwise provided in this
2 section and sections 162.492 and 162.563, all elections of
3 school directors in urban school districts shall be held
4 biennially at the same times and places as municipal
5 elections.
6 2. Except as otherwise provided in subsections 3, 4,
7 [and] 5, and 7 of this section, hereafter when a seven-
8 director district becomes an urban school district, the
9 directors of the prior seven-director district shall
10 continue as directors of the urban school district until the
11 expiration of the terms for which they were elected and
12 until their successors are elected as provided in this
13 subsection. The first biennial school election for
14 directors shall be held in the urban school district at the
15 time provided in subsection 1 which is on the date of or
16 subsequent to the expiration of the terms of the directors

52 5. In any school district in any county with a charter

67 elections shall be held annually at the same times and
68 places as general municipal elections for all years where
69 one or more terms expire, and upon expiration of any term
70 after August 28, 2015, the term of office shall be for three
71 years and until their successors are duly elected and
72 qualified.
73 7. In any school district in which a majority of the
74 district is located in a city with more than one hundred
75 five thousand but fewer than one hundred twenty-five

1 167.1001. 1. For purposes of this section, the
2 following terms mean:
3 (1) "Election", any primary, general, or special
4 election held to nominate or elect an individual to public
5 office, to retain or recall an elected officeholder, or to
6 submit a ballot measure to the voters;
7 (2) "Parent", a student's parent, legal guardian, or
8 person standing in loco parentis to the student;
9 (3) "School", any public school governed and managed
10 by an urban school district.
11 2. A student shall not be considered absent from the
12 school in which the student is enrolled for the time during
13 which the student accompanies his or her parent when the
14 parent is exercising his or her right to vote in a scheduled
15 election.
16 3. A student may receive an excused absence pursuant
17 to subsection 2 of this section only one time during each
18 scheduled election.
19 4. A student who accompanies his or her parent when
20 the parent is exercising his or her right to vote in a
21 scheduled election shall be encouraged by school officials
22 to demonstrate his or her attendance at the scheduled
23 election by wearing an official sticker, badge, or other
24 item upon his or her return to school indicating that he or
25 she attended the scheduled election.
