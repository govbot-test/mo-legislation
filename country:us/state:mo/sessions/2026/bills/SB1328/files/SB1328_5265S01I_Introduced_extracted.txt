SECOND REGULAR SESSION
SENATE BILL NO. 1328
103RD GENERA L ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY SENATOR LEWIS.
5265S.01I KRISTINA MARTIN, Secretary
AN ACT
To amend chapter 9, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to blood donor awareness
month.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Missouri, as follows:
1 Section A. Chapter 9, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto
2 one new section, to be known as section 9.025, to read as
3 follows:
1 9.025. The month of January is hereby designated as
2 "Blood Donor Awareness Month". It is recommended to the
3 people of this state that the month be appropriately
4 observed through activities that increase awareness of the
5 importance of blood donation. The state deems blood and
6 blood products to be a critical resource and a vital public
7 health asset that must be readily available at all times.
