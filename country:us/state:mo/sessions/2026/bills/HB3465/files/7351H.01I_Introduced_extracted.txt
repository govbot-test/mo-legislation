SECOND REGULAR SESSION
HOUSE BILL NO. 3465
103RD GENERAL ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY REPRESENTATIVE KEATHLEY.
7351H.01I JOSEPH ENGLER, Chief Clerk
AN ACT
To amend chapter 1, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to the severability of
provisions within legislation.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
Section A. Chapter 1, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto one new section, to be
2 known as section 1.141, to read as follows:
1.141. If any provision of a legislative act is found unconstitutional by a court of
2 competent jurisdiction for any reason, whether procedural or substantive, the provision
3 shall be severed, the remaining provisions shall be valid, and the court shall presume
4 that the general assembly would have enacted the valid provisions without the void one,
5 provided that if the court finds by clear and convincing evidence that the general
6 assembly would not have enacted the valid provisions absent the void provision, then the
7 valid provisions shall also be declared invalid. Clear and convincing evidence shall
8 include, but not be limited to, a finding that the valid provisions, standing alone, are
9 incomplete and are incapable of being executed in accordance with the legislative intent.
