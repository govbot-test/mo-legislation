AN ACT
To repeal section 137.016, RSMo, and to enact in lieu
thereof one new section relating to classification of
certain residential real property.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Missouri, as follows:
1 Section A. Section 137.016, RSMo, is repealed and one new
2 section enacted in lieu thereof, to be known as section 137.016,
3 to read as follows:
1 137.016. 1. As used in Section 4(b) of Article X of
2 the Missouri Constitution, the following terms mean:
3 (1) "Residential property"[,]:
4 (a) All real property improved by a structure which is
5 used or intended to be used for residential living by human
6 occupants[,];
7 (b) Vacant land in connection with an airport[,];
8 (c) Land used as a golf course[,];
9 (d) Manufactured home parks[,];

12 rent[, and];

15 subject to sales tax under subdivision (6) of subsection 1

18 business that is leased for a term of less than thirty
19 consecutive days, in whole or in part, subject to sales tax

39 sales tax pursuant to subdivision (6) of subsection 1 of
