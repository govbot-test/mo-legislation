AN ACT
To repeal sections 340.200, 340.216, 340.220, and 340.222, RSMo, and to enact in lieu thereof
five new sections relating to livestock reproductive specialists.

13 (3) "Animal", any wild, exotic or domestic, living or

1 340.220. It is considered the practice of veterinary
2 medicine to use any invasive procedure to remove any embryo
3 or oocyte from an animal for the purpose of transplanting
4 such embryo or fertilized oocyte into another female animal
5 or for the purpose of cryopreserving such embryo, or to
6 implant such embryo into an animal. It is not considered
7 the practice of veterinary medicine for:
8 (1) A person or that person's full-time employees to
9 remove an embryo from the person's own animal for the
10 purpose of transplanting or cryopreserving such embryo or to
11 implant an embryo into the person's own animal; however,
12 ownership of the animal shall not be transferred or the
13 employment of any person changed for the purpose of
14 circumventing sections 340.200 to 340.330; or
15 (2) A livestock reproductive specialist trained in
16 advanced reproductive technologies to remove an embryo or
17 oocyte from livestock for the purpose of transplanting such
18 embryo into another female livestock animal or for the
19 purpose of cryopreserving such embryo, or to implant such
20 embryo into a livestock animal while under indirect
21 supervision of a licensed veterinarian.
