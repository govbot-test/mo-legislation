SECOND REGULAR SESSION
HOUSE BILL NO. 3342
103RD GENERAL ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY REPRESENTATIVE MATTHIESEN.
6907H.01I JOSEPH ENGLER, Chief Clerk
AN ACT
To amend chapter 1, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to certain property
taxes applicable to motor vehicles.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
Section A. Chapter 1, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto one new section, to be
2 known as section 1.2070, to read as follows:
1.2070. Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, no personal
2 property tax levy passed by voters before January 1, 1975, shall apply to motor vehicles.
✔
EXPLANATION — Matter enclosed in bold-faced brackets [thus] in the above bill is not enacted and is
intended to be omitted from the law. Matter in bold-face type in the above bill is proposed language.
