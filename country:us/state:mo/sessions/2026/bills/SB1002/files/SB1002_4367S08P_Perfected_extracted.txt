AN ACT
To repeal sections 162.301, 162.341, 162.459, and 162.481, RSMo, and to enact in lieu thereof six
new sections relating to elections submitted to the people by a school board.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Missouri, as follows:
1 Section A. Sections 162.301, 162.341, 162.459, and
2 162.481, RSMo, are repealed and six new sections enacted in
3 lieu thereof, to be known as sections 162.082, 162.301, 162.341,
4 162.459, 162.481, and 164.320, to read as follows:
1 162.082. 1. Elections for all directors of any school
2 district in which a majority of the district is located in a
3 county with more than four hundred thousand but fewer than
4 five hundred thousand inhabitants shall be held on the first
5 Tuesday after the first Monday in November of even-numbered
6 years, and the term of every school board member elected in
7 2028 and every year thereafter shall be four years,
8 notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary.
9 2. Any school board member whose term expires prior to
10 the first Tuesday after the first Monday in November of 2028
11 or in any other odd-numbered year thereafter shall continue
12 to serve until his or her successor is duly elected and
13 qualified at the next general election.
