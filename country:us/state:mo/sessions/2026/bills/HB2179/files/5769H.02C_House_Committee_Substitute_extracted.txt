SECOND REGULAR SESSION
HOUSE COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE FOR
103RD GENERAL ASSEMBLY
5769H.02C JOSEPH ENGLER, Chief Clerk
AN ACT
To amend chapter 493, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to the publication
of public notices.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:

Section 2:
Section A. Chapter 493, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto one new section, to be
2 known as section 493.220, to read as follows:

Section 3:
493.220. 1. Notwithstanding any other provision of law, beginning on the
2 effective date of this section, in any county with a charter form of government, any
3 notice required by statute to be published in a newspaper shall also be published on the
4 website of the office of the secretary of state or published in a newspaper.
5 2. The office of the secretary of state shall develop procedures for submission of
6 notices published on its website as provided under this section and create a specific page
7 on its website that contains all such notices in a searchable format that is easily
8 accessible to the public.
9 3. Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, any political
10 subdivision or governmental entity that is required by law to publish a public
11 advertisement, order of publication, legal notice, or any other notice in a newspaper
12 shall be permitted to publish the identical notice on its official website and on any
13 official social media accounts it maintains for public notices. In addition to the
14 newspaper publication or if no qualifying newspaper exists in the jurisdiction or the
15 newspaper fails or refuses to publish the notice after timely submission and proper
16 payment, if any, such digital publication shall constitute valid and sufficient notice upon
