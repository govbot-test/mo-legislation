SECOND REGULAR SESSION
HOUSE BILL NO. 1747
103RD GENERAL ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY REPRESENTATIVE MILLER.
4019H.01I JOSEPH ENGLER, Chief Clerk
AN ACT
To amend chapter 537, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to civil actions for
certain damages.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
Section A. Chapter 537, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto one new section, to be
2 known as section 537.041, to read as follows:
537.041. 1. As used in this section, "artificial intelligence" means an engineered
2 or machine-based system that varies in its level of autonomy and that can, for explicit or
3 implicit objectives, infer from the input it receives how to generate outputs that can
4 influence physical or virtual environments.
5 2. Any image, video, or audio that is generated or modified using artificial
6 intelligence shall contain a mark or statement that is visible or audible to users that the
7 image, video, or audio is generated or modified using artificial intelligence. Such mark
8 or statement shall be developed by the secretary of state, and such mark or statement
9 shall be used by all persons or entities publishing or disseminating any image, video, or
10 audio generated or modified using artificial intelligence.
11 3. Any person who has suffered injury for a violation of subsection 2 of this
12 section shall have standing to bring a cause of action against the publisher or
13 disseminator of the image, video, or audio for damages.
