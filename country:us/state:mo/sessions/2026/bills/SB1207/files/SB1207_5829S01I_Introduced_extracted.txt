SECOND REGULAR SESSION
SENATE BILL NO. 1207
103RD GENERA L ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY SENATOR GREGORY (21).
5829S.01I KRISTINA MARTIN, Secretary
AN ACT
To repeal section 173.445, RSMo, and to enact in lieu thereof one new section relating to higher
education.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Missouri, as follows:
1 Section A. Section 173.445, RSMo, is repealed and one new
2 section enacted in lieu thereof, to be known as section 173.445,
3 to read as follows:
1 173.445. The higher education loan authority is
2 assigned to the department of higher education and workforce
3 development. The authority shall annually file with the
4 director of said department and with the joint committee on
5 education a report of its previous year's income,
6 expenditures and bonds or other forms of indebtedness issued
7 and outstanding.
