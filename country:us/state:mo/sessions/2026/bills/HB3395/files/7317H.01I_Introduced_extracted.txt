AN ACT
To amend chapter 99, RSMo, by adding thereto six new sections relating to incentives for
downtown redevelopment.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
Section A. Chapter 99, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto six new sections, to be
2 known as sections 99.1250, 99.1255, 99.1260, 99.1265, 99.1270, and 99.1275, to read as
3 follows:
99.1250. 1. Sections 99.1250 to 99.1275 shall be known and may be cited as the
2 "Missouri Downtown Economic Stimulus Act Extension Act".
3 2. The general assembly finds and declares that:
4 (1) The Missouri downtown and rural economic stimulus act ("MODESA")
5 established a highly successful economic development program that resulted in
6 landmark urban revitalization developments in the state's largest metropolitan areas;
7 (2) MODESA-approved development projects in Kansas City and St. Louis
8 draw over twenty million visitors a year and are among the most visited destinations by
9 tourists to the state;
10 (3) MODESA incentivized over two billion dollars in private investment in the
11 MODESA-approved development projects, transforming the state's urban cores;
12 (4) MODESA-approved development projects have generated thousands of jobs
13 and provided billions of dollars in economic impact;
14 (5) MODESA-approved development projects have successfully drawn
15 thousands of new residents to the state, and have spurred substantial additional
16 private development in the state;
