JOINT RESOLUTION
Submitting to the qualified voters of Missouri an amendment repealing Section 38(b) of
Article III of the Constitution of Missouri, and adopting one new section in lieu
thereof relating to the blind pension fund.
Be it resolved by the House of Representatives, the Senate concurring therein:
That at the next general election to be held in the state of Missouri, on Tuesday next
2 following the first Monday in November, 2026, or at a special election to be called by the
3 governor for that purpose, there is hereby submitted to the qualified voters of this state, for
4 adoption or rejection, the following amendment to Article III of the Constitution of the state
5 of Missouri:
Section A. Section 38(b), Article III, Constitution of Missouri, is repealed and one
2 new section adopted in lieu thereof, to be known as Section 38(b), to read as follows:
Section 38(b). The general assembly shall provide an annual [tax of not less than one-
2 half of one cent nor more than three cents on the one hundred dollars valuation of all taxable
3 property to be levied and collected as other taxes,] appropriation for the purpose of
4 providing a fund to be appropriated and used for the pensioning of the deserving blind as
5 provided by law. The amount appropriated each year shall be at least the same amount
6 the general assembly appropriated to the fund for the 2026-27 fiscal year. Any balance
7 remaining in the fund after the payment of the pensions may be appropriated for the adequate
