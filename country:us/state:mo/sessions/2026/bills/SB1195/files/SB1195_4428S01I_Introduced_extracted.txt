SECOND REGULAR SESSION
SENATE BILL NO. 1195
103RD GENERA L ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY SENATOR HENDERSON.
4428S.01I KRISTINA MARTIN, Secretary
AN ACT
To amend chapter 321, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to fire protection district
surplus property.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Missouri, as follows:
1 Section A. Chapter 321, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto
2 one new section, to be known as section 321.295, to read as
3 follows:
1 321.295. 1. The board of directors of a fire
2 protection district may distribute surplus or unneeded
3 supplies or property to volunteer fire protection
4 associations, as defined in section 320.300, to fire
5 protection districts, to fire departments, and to eligible
6 donees, as such term is defined in connection with the
7 federal surplus property program, in the same manner as
8 provided for the distribution of federal surplus property.
9 The board may distribute surplus or unneeded supplies or
10 property to an organization registered as a not-for-profit
11 organization pursuant to 26 U.S.C. Section 501(c)3, public
12 service corporation that provides training to fire
13 departments, emergency medical technicians, or police
14 officers in search and recovery techniques, water rescue,
15 ice rescue, or watercraft operation in the same manner as
16 provided for the distribution of federal surplus property.

SB 1195 2
17 2. The board of directors of a fire protection
18 district may sell surplus or unneeded supplies or property
19 that are not transferred to fire protection associations,
20 districts, or departments or eligible donees to the general
21 public by auction, sealed bid.
22 3. Notwithstanding the provisions of subsections 1 and
23 2 of this section to the contrary, the board of directors of
24 a fire protection district may not donate or sell surplus
25 emergency vehicles or unneeded supplies or property to board
26 members, officers, or employees of the fire protection
27 district or their relatives within the fourth degree, by
28 consanguinity or affinity, nor to an entity of which a board
29 member, officer, or employee is a board member or officer,
30 unless such surplus is sold on an online auction platform
31 approved by the Missouri Office of Administration.
