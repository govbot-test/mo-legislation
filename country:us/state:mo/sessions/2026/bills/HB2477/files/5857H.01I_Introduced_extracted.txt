103RD GENERAL ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY REPRESENTATIVE LEWIS.
5857H.01I JOSEPH ENGLER, Chief Clerk
AN ACT
To amend chapter 393, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to moratorium on
the construction of solar projects, with an emergency clause.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
Section A. Chapter 393, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto one new section, to be
2 known as section 393.2180, to read as follows:
393.2180. 1. For purposes of this section, the following terms mean:
2 (1) "Department", the Missouri department of natural resources;
3 (2) "Solar project", a group of photovoltaic interconnected solar panels or
4 arrays that convert sunlight into electricity for the primary purpose of wholesale or
5 retail sales of generated electricity, including all on-site equipment and facilities
6 necessary for the proper operation of the facility, such as electrical collection and
7 transmission lines, battery storage systems, transformers, substations, and operations
8 and maintenance facilities.
9 2. Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, beginning the effective
10 date of this section, there shall immediately be a moratorium placed on the construction
11 of any solar project in the state, including but not limited to the issuance of permits for
12 the construction of solar projects in the state.
13 3. For any solar project that is currently being constructed and for which
14 construction has not completed before the effective date of this section, all construction
15 of such solar project shall be suspended under the moratorium under this section.
16 4. The department shall promulgate rules relating to environmental issues
17 concerning, but not limited to, the construction, placement, and operation of a solar
