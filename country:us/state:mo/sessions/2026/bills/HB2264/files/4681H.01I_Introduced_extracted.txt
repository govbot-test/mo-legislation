4681H.01I JOSEPH ENGLER, Chief Clerk
AN ACT
To amend chapter 9, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to election judges and
poll workers appreciation day.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:

Section 2:
Section A. Chapter 9, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto one new section, to be
2 known as section 9.410, to read as follows:

Section 3:
9.410. March sixteenth each year is hereby designated as "Election Judges and
2 Poll Workers Appreciation Day" in Missouri. The citizens of this state are encouraged
3 to participate in appropriate events and activities to thank and celebrate the election
4 judges and poll workers who ensure the functioning of our republic.
