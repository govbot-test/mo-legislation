SECOND REGULAR SESSION
SENATE BILL NO. 1489
103RD GENERA L ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY SENATOR GREGORY (21).
6142S.01I KRISTINA MARTIN, Secretary
AN ACT
To amend chapter 195, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to the over-the-counter
purchase of certain medications, with an emergency clause.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Missouri, as follows:
1 Section A. Chapter 195, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto
2 one new section, to be known as section 195.1000, to read as
3 follows:
1 195.1000. Notwithstanding any other provision of law
2 to the contrary, ivermectin tablets and hydroxychloroquine
3 tablets shall be available to the public through over-the-
4 counter purchase in this state without a prescription or
5 consultation with a pharmacist or other health care
6 professional.
1 Section B. Because immediate action is necessary to
2 ensure the public has adequate access to ivermectin tablets
3 and hydroxychloroquine tablets, section A of this act is
4 deemed necessary for the immediate preservation of the
5 public health, welfare, peace, and safety, and is hereby
6 declared to be an emergency act within the meaning of the
7 constitution, and section A of this act shall be in full
8 force and effect upon its passage and approval.
