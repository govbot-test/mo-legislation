SECOND REGULAR SESSION
SENATE BILL NO. 1617
103RD GENERA L ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY SENATOR BRATTIN.
6960S.01I KRISTINA MARTIN, Secretary
AN ACT
To amend chapter 173, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to accountability for
low-earning outcome degrees in higher education.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Missouri, as follows:
1 Section A. Chapter 173, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto
2 one new section, to be known as section 173.031, to read as
3 follows:
1 173.031. 1. This section shall be known and may be
2 cited as the "Accountability for Low-Earning Outcome Degrees
3 in Higher Education Act of 2026".
4 2. The coordinating board for higher education shall:
5 (1) Adopt rules to prohibit state funds from being
6 allocated or expended, either directly or indirectly, for
7 academic degree programs that satisfy the definition of a
8 low-earning outcome program under Section 84001 of Pub. L.
9 119-21. These restrictions shall include, but are not
10 limited to, the following categories of higher education
11 funding:
12 (a) Individual student financial aid;
13 (b) Base operational or instructional funding;
14 (c) Separately or specially appropriated aid or
15 grants; and
16 (d) Capital or facilities funding;

SB 1617 2
17 (2) Annually review updated federal determinations of
18 low-earning outcome programs under Section 84001 of Pub. L.
19 119-21 and adopt rules in accordance with subdivision (1) of
20 this subsection; and
21 (3) Submit an annual and publicly available report to
22 the general assembly documenting:
23 (a) Programs prohibited from receiving state aid under
24 this section;
25 (b) Enforcement of such prohibitions; and
26 (c) The estimated fiscal impact due to the
27 prohibitions.
