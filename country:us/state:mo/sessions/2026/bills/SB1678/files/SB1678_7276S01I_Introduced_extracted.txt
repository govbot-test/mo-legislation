AN ACT
To amend chapter 135, RSMo, by adding thereto three new sections relating to tax credits for child
care.

60 January 1, 2027, a taxpayer may claim the tax credit

99 more child care providers within two years of receipt.

101 intermediary to timely issue the contribution verification

103 the taxpayer to a refund of the contribution from the child
104 care provider or intermediary.

108 (1) The contribution is used directly by the child
