Section 1:
SECOND REGULAR SESSION
103RD GENERA L ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY SENATOR TRENT.
6037S.01I KRISTINA MARTIN, Secretary
AN ACT
To amend chapter 168, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to school employees.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Missouri, as follows:
1 Section A. Chapter 168, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto
2 one new section, to be known as section 168.780, to read as
3 follows:
1 168.780. 1. Personally identifiable information of a
2 teacher collected by a public school or school district
3 shall not be sold or disclosed by any public school, school
4 district, or third-party entity to any party for any
5 commercial use, including, but not limited to, marketing or
6 solicitation; compiling, selling, or renting lists;
7 developing or enhancing products or services; or creating or
8 using individual, household, or group profiles.
9 2. If a public school or school district conducts a
10 background check on a teacher for purposes relating to such
11 teacher's work-related travel or transportation of students
12 for educational purposes, under no circumstances shall such
13 background check be reported, transmitted, or otherwise
14 treated as a credit inquiry or have any direct or indirect
15 effect on the teacher's credit score.
✓
