AN ACT
To repeal section 526.010 as enacted by senate bill no. 22, one hundred third general
assembly, first regular session, and section 526.010 as enacted by senate bill no. 1211,
ninety-second general assembly, second regular session, and to enact in lieu thereof
one new section relating to preliminary injunctions in judicial proceedings.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
Section A. Section 526.010 as enacted by senate bill no. 22, one hundred third general
2 assembly, first regular session, and section 526.010 as enacted by senate bill no. 1211, ninety-
3 second general assembly, second regular session, are repealed and one new section enacted in
4 lieu thereof, to be known as section 526.010, to read as follows:
[526.010. 1. Injunctions may be granted by a circuit judge or an
2 associate circuit judge.
3 2. In any action in which the state or a statewide official is
4 preliminarily enjoined from implementing, enforcing, or otherwise
5 effectuating any provision of the Constitution of Missouri, any Missouri
6 statute, or any Missouri regulation, the attorney general may appeal the
7 preliminary injunction.
8 3. The attorney general may appeal any preliminary injunction that
9 existed prior to August 28, 2025, under subsection 2 of this section.]
