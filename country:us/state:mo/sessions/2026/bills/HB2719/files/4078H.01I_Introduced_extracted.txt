SECOND REGULAR SESSION
103RD GENERAL ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY REPRESENTATIVE DOUGLAS.
4078H.01I JOSEPH ENGLER, Chief Clerk
AN ACT
To repeal section 376.620, RSMo, and to enact in lieu thereof one new section relating to life
insurance.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:

Section 2:
Section A. Section 376.620, RSMo, is repealed and one new section enacted in lieu
2 thereof, to be known as section 376.620, to read as follows:

Section 3:
376.620. 1. Any life insurance policy, rider, endorsement, amendment, or certificate
2 issued or delivered in this state may exclude or restrict liability under such policy, rider,
3 endorsement, amendment, or certificate for death as the result of suicide in the event the
4 insured, while sane or insane, dies as a result of suicide within [one year] two years from the
5 date of the issue of such policy, rider, endorsement, amendment, or certificate. If an insured
6 applies for additional death benefits or an increase in death benefits after initial coverage
7 commences, the policy, rider, endorsement, amendment, or certificate may provide for an
8 exclusion for suicide that occurs within [one year] two years after any addition or increase in
9 death benefits only to the extent of the additional or increased death benefits. Any such
10 exclusion or restriction shall be clearly stated in such policy, rider, endorsement, amendment,
11 or certificate.
