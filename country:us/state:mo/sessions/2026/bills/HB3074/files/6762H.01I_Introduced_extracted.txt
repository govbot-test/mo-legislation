SECOND REGULAR SESSION
HOUSE BILL NO. 3074
103RD GENERAL ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY REPRESENTATIVE FOUNTAIN HENDERSON.
6762H.01I JOSEPH ENGLER, Chief Clerk
AN ACT
To amend chapter 9, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to William Lacy Clay
Sr. day.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
Section A. Chapter 9, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto one new section, to be
2 known as section 9.509, to read as follows:
9.509. April thirtieth each year is hereby designated as "William Lacy Clay Sr.
2 Day" in Missouri. The citizens of this state are encouraged to participate in appropriate
3 events and activities to celebrate the life and legacy of William Lacy Clay Sr., who
4 represented Missouri's first district in Congress from 1969 to 2001 and was one of the
5 founding members of the Congressional Black Caucus.
