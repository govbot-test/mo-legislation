AN ACT
To repeal section 67.652, RSMo, and to enact in lieu thereof one new section relating to the
regional convention and sports complex authority.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
Section A. Section 67.652, RSMo, is repealed and one new section enacted in lieu
