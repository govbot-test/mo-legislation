AN ACT
To amend chapter 115, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to maintenance of
voter rolls.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:

Section 2:
Section A. Chapter 115, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto one new section, to be
2 known as section 115.028, to read as follows:

Section 3:
115.028. 1. The department of health and senior services shall, on a monthly
2 basis, provide the secretary of state with a report of every death certificate filed with the
3 department in the preceding month.
4 2. The secretary of state shall, within ten days of receipt of the report required
5 under subsection 1 of this section, notify appropriate local election authorities of the
6 deaths.
7 3. The local election authority shall, within ten days of receipt of the notification
8 required under subsection 2 of this section, remove from its voter rolls any names
9 received from the secretary of the state.
