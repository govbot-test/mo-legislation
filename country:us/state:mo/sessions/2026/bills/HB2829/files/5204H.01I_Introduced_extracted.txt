SECOND REGULAR SESSION
HOUSE BILL NO. 2829
103RD GENERAL ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY REPRESENTATIVE WOODS.
5204H.01I JOSEPH ENGLER, Chief Clerk
AN ACT
To amend chapter 407, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to arbitration
agreements.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
Section A. Chapter 407, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto one new section, to be
2 known as section 407.028, to read as follows:
407.028. No claim brought under sections 407.010 to 407.315 shall be subject to
2 arbitration unless both parties agree to arbitration in a separate written agreement.
✔
EXPLANATION — Matter enclosed in bold-faced brackets [thus] in the above bill is not enacted and is
intended to be omitted from the law. Matter in bold-face type in the above bill is proposed language.
