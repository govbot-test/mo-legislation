SECOND REGULAR SESSION
HOUSE BILL NO. 3535
103RD GENERAL ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY REPRESENTATIVE COOK.
7525H.01I JOSEPH ENGLER, Chief Clerk
AN ACT
To repeal section 320.230, RSMo, and to enact in lieu thereof one new section relating to
peace officer duties.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
Section A. Section 320.230, RSMo, is repealed and one new section enacted in lieu
2 thereof, to be known as section 320.230, to read as follows:
320.230. 1. The state fire marshal shall conduct investigations and may conduct
2 hearings into the cause, origin, or circumstances of fire losses and shall cooperate with the
3 appropriate fire or police officials of this state or its political subdivisions in investigations of
4 the cause, origin or circumstances of fires, explosions or related occurrences involving the
5 possibility of arson or related offenses.
6 2. The state fire marshal and the marshal's paid investigators who have attained
7 proper certification as peace officers pursuant to chapter 590 as approved by the director of
8 the department of public safety shall be deemed to be peace officers and shall notify the local
9 law enforcement agency of the existence of the investigators and shall have the power of
10 arrest for all offenses incident to the investigation of the cause, origin or circumstances of
11 fires, fireworks, explosions or like occurrences involving the possibility of arson, or related
12 offenses, and in connection with any offenses when aiding and assisting the sheriff of any
13 county or the chief of police of any municipality, or their designated representatives, at their
14 request, or when exigent circumstances that present an immediate threat of bodily harm
15 to any person exist.
