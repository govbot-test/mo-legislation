JOINT RESOLUTION
Submitting to the qualified voters of Missouri an amendment repealing Section 36(c) of
Article IV of the Constitution of Missouri, and adopting one new section in lieu
thereof relating to MO HealthNet.
Be it resolved by the House of Representatives, the Senate concurring therein:
That at the next general election to be held in the state of Missouri, on Tuesday next
2 following the first Monday in November, 2026, or at a special election to be called by the
3 governor for that purpose, there is hereby submitted to the qualified voters of this state, for
4 adoption or rejection, the following amendment to Article IV of the Constitution of the state
5 of Missouri:
Section A. Section 36(c), Article IV, Constitution of Missouri, is repealed and one
2 new section adopted in lieu thereof, to be known as Section 36(c), to read as follows:
Section 36(c). 1. Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, beginning
2 July 1, 2021, individuals nineteen years of age or older and under sixty-five years of age who
3 qualify for MO HealthNet services under 42 U.S.C. Section 1396a(a)(10)(A)(i)(VIII) and as
4 set forth in 42 C.F.R. 435.119, and who have income at or below one hundred thirty-three
5 percent of the federal poverty level plus five percent of the applicable family size as
6 determined under 42 U.S.C. Section 1396a(e)(14) and as set forth in 42 C.F.R. 435.603, shall
7 be eligible for medical assistance under MO HealthNet and shall receive coverage for the
8 health benefits service package.
