AN ACT
To amend chapter 393, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to transfers of
utility service under certain circumstances.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
Section A. Chapter 393, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto one new section, to be
2 known as section 393.1685, to read as follows:
393.1685. 1. No public utility, as such term is defined in section 295.020, shall
2 require additional deposits or transfer fees to transfer services to the spouse of the
3 account holder when the account is in good standing and the account holder:
4 (1) Dies; or
5 (2) Is incarcerated for more than sixty days.
6 2. (1) Notwithstanding the provisions of subsection 1 of this section, a new
7 deposit may be required if:
8 (a) The spouse has an unpaid bill within the last five years that, at the time of the
9 transfer request, remains unpaid and is not the subject of a dispute with any public
10 utility for the same type of service;
11 (b) The spouse has, in the last five years and without authorization, interfered
12 with or diverted the service of a public utility for the same type of service; or
13 (c) The spouse fails to provide proof of identity, such as government-issued photo
14 identification and Social Security number.
15 (2) If a new deposit is required under the provisions of this subsection, the public
16 utility shall permit the spouse to pay the deposit in installments unless the public utility
