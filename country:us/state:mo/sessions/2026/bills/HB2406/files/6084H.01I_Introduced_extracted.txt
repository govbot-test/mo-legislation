AN ACT
To amend chapter 536, RSMo, by adding thereto two new sections relating to administrative
rules.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
Section A. Chapter 536, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto two new sections, to be
2 known as sections 536.180 and 536.185, to read as follows:
536.180. 1. Any state agency filing a notice of proposed rulemaking, as required
2 by section 536.021, wherein the adoption, amendment, or rescission of the rule would, as
3 determined by the fiscal note required under this chapter, require or result in an
4 expenditure of public funds by that agency, any other state agency, or any political
5 subdivision of the state in excess of two hundred fifty thousand dollars per year, or
6 would require or result in an expenditure of money by or a reduction in income for
7 individuals or businesses in excess of two hundred fifty thousand dollars per year shall,
8 in addition to the requirements of section 536.200, file notification with the joint
9 committee on administrative rules of such proposed rule. The committee may hold
10 hearings on any proposed rule or portion thereof at any time. A final order of
11 rulemaking shall not be filed with the secretary of state until at least thirty days after
12 such final order of rulemaking has been received by the committee.
13 2. Any proposed rule subject to the provisions of this section shall not be subject
14 to section 536.021 and shall not become effective until the provisions of this section have
15 been satisfied.
16 3. A proposed rule subject to the provisions of this section shall not become
17 effective unless and until the general assembly adopts a concurrent resolution in
