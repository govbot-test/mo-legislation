SECOND REGULAR SESSION
SENATE BILL NO. 1290
103RD GENERA L ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY SENATOR HUDSON.
4339S.01I KRISTINA MARTIN, Secretary
AN ACT
To amend chapter 167, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to ultraprocessed food
in schools.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Missouri, as follows:
1 Section A. Chapter 167, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto
2 one new section, to be known as section 167.207, to read as
3 follows:
1 167.207. 1. This section shall be known and may be
2 cited as the "Missouri Healthy Schools Act".
3 2. For the purposes of this section, "ultraprocessed
4 food" means a food or beverage that contains one or more of
5 the following ingredients:
6 (1) Potassium bromate;
7 (2) Propylparaben;
8 (3) Titanium dioxide;
9 (4) Brominated vegetable oil;
10 (5) Yellow dye 5;
11 (6) Yellow dye 6;
12 (7) Blue dye 1;
13 (8) Blue dye 2;
14 (9) Green dye 3;
15 (10) Red dye 3;
16 (11) Red dye 40.

SB 1290 2
17 3. Beginning in the 2027-2028 school year and for all
18 subsequent school years, any school that participates in a
19 federally funded or assisted meal program shall not serve,
20 sell, or allow a third party to sell ultraprocessed food on
21 the school campus during the school day. This subsection
22 shall not be construed to prohibit a student's parent or
23 guardian from providing ultraprocessed food to the student
24 during the school day.
25 4. The department of elementary and secondary
26 education shall post on the department's website both of the
27 following:
28 (1) A standardized form that a public school may use
29 to certify that the public school is complying with the
30 requirements of this section; and
31 (2) A list of each public school that has certified to
32 the department that the public school is complying with the
33 requirements of this section.
