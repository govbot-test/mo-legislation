SECOND REGULAR SESSION
SENATE BILL NO. 942
103RD GENERA L ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY SENATOR BROWN (16).
4551S.01I KRISTINA MARTIN, Secretary
AN ACT
To amend chapter 304, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to vehicle weight limits.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Missouri, as follows:
1 Section A. Chapter 304, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto
2 one new section, to be known as section 304.173, to read as
3 follows:
1 304.173. 1. As used in this section, the following
2 terms mean:
3 (1) "Construction materials", materials intended for
4 use in a construction project, including, but not limited
5 to, asphalt, concrete, and aggregates;
6 (2) "Interstate system", that portion of the national
7 system of interstate highways located within the boundaries
8 of Missouri, as officially designated or may be hereafter
9 designated by the state highways and transportation
10 commission with the approval of the Secretary of
11 Transportation, pursuant to Title 23, United States Code, as
12 amended;
13 (3) "Raw materials", materials intended for use to
14 produce construction materials, including, but not limited
15 to, sand, gravel, and rock.
16 2. Subject to the requirements specified in this
17 section, vehicles transporting construction materials from
18 the site of their production to the site of a construction

SB 942 2
19 project at which the materials will be used, or transporting
20 raw materials from their point of origin to a site at which
21 construction materials are produced, may exceed the weight
22 limits specified in section 304.180 by up to ten percent,
23 provided that the vehicle is properly maintained and
24 equipped to operate at the excess weight safely, and does
25 not exceed the gross vehicle weight rating for the vehicle
26 as specified by the vehicle's manufacturer.
27 3. This section shall not apply with regard to the
28 interstate system.
29 4. No vehicle operated at an excess weight in
30 accordance with this section shall be operated on any bridge
31 or other structure with a posted weight limit lower than the
32 vehicle's gross vehicle weight.
33 5. No vehicle operated at an excess weight in
34 accordance with this section shall be operated on any bridge
35 or other structure with any posted weight limit at a speed
36 greater than thirty miles per hour.
