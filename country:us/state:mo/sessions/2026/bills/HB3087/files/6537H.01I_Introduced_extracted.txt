SECOND REGULAR SESSION
HOUSE BILL NO. 3087
103RD GENERAL ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY REPRESENTATIVE DIEHL.
6537H.01I JOSEPH ENGLER, Chief Clerk
AN ACT
To amend chapter 262, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to the classification
of certain plants.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
Section A. Chapter 262, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto one new section, to be
2 known as section 262.975, to read as follows:
262.975. Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, for the purposes
2 of chapter 89, Helianthus annuus or any variety of Helianthus annuus shall not be
3 considered an agricultural crop.
✔
EXPLANATION — Matter enclosed in bold-faced brackets [thus] in the above bill is not enacted and is
intended to be omitted from the law. Matter in bold-face type in the above bill is proposed language.
