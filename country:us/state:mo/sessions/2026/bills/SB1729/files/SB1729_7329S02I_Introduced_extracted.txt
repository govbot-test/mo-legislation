AN ACT
To repeal section 160.572, RSMo, and to enact in lieu thereof two new sections relating to college
and career readiness.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Missouri, as follows:
1 Section A. Section 160.572, RSMo, is repealed and two new
2 sections enacted in lieu thereof, to be known as sections
3 160.572 and 173.1360, to read as follows:
1 160.572. 1. For purposes of this section, the
2 following terms shall mean:
3 (1) ["ACT assessment", the ACT assessment or the ACT
4 Plus Writing assessment;
5 (2)] "High school", a public school, not including a
6 charter school, that provides instruction to students in any
7 combinations of grades nine, ten, eleven, or twelve;
8 (2) "WorkKeys", the ACT WorkKeys assessments required
9 for the National Career Readiness Certificate.
10 2. [(1) In any school year in which the department of
11 elementary and secondary education directs a state-funded
12 census administration of the ACT assessment to any group of
13 students, any student who would be allowed or required to
14 participate in the census administration shall receive the
15 opportunity, on any date within three months before the
16 census administration, to participate in a state-funded
17 administration of WorkKeys.
