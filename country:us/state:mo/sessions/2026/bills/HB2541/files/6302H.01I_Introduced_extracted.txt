SECOND REGULAR SESSION
HOUSE BILL NO. 2541
103RD GENERAL ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY REPRESENTATIVE CROSSLEY.
6302H.01I JOSEPH ENGLER, Chief Clerk
AN ACT
To amend chapter 9, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to Samuel Dale
Brownback awareness day.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
Section A. Chapter 9, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto one new section, to be
2 known as section 9.505, to read as follows:
9.505. April fifteenth is hereby designated as "Samuel Dale Brownback
2 Awareness Day" in Missouri. The citizens of this state, especially members of the
3 general assembly, are encouraged to participate in appropriate events and activities to
4 reflect on the well-documented fiscal and economic consequences experienced in
5 neighboring states following the aggressive elimination of income taxes, including
6 persistent budget deficits, credit downgrades, cuts to public education and
7 infrastructure, and subsequent bipartisan legislative action taken to reverse such
8 policies, in order to promote prudent and evidence-based tax policy decisions in
9 Missouri.
