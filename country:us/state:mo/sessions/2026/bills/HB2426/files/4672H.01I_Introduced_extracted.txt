AN ACT
To repeal section 431.061, RSMo, and to enact in lieu thereof three new sections relating to
parental rights.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
Section A. Section 431.061, RSMo, is repealed and three new sections enacted in lieu
2 thereof, to be known as sections 1.390, 1.391, and 431.061, to read as follows:
1.390. 1. As used in this section, the following terms mean:
2 (1) "Child", any unemancipated person who has not attained eighteen years of
3 age;
4 (2) "Parent", a natural parent, a parent by adoption, or an individual who has
5 been granted exclusive right and authority over the welfare of a child under state law.
6 2. A parent has a fundamental liberty interest in the care, custody, and control of
7 the parent's child, which includes the right to direct the upbringing, education, health
8 care, and mental health care of the child free from government interference.
9 3. A governmental authority shall not restrict the fundamental right of a parent
10 to direct the upbringing, education, health care, or mental health care of that parent's
11 child unless the governmental authority demonstrates that application of the restriction
12 to the parent and the child is essential to further a compelling governmental interest and
13 is the least-restrictive means of furthering that compelling governmental interest.
14 4. This section applies to all state and local laws, resolutions, and ordinances and
15 the implementation of such laws, resolutions, and ordinances, whether statutory or
16 otherwise, and whether adopted before, on, or after August 28, 2026.
