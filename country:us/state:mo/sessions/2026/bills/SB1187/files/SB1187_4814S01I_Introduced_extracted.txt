SECOND REGULAR SESSION
SENATE BILL NO. 1187
103RD GENERA L ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY SENATOR COLEMAN.
4814S.01I KRISTINA MARTIN, Secretary
AN ACT
To amend chapter 579, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to the offense of
consumption of marijuana in a public space, with penalty provisions.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Missouri, as follows:
1 Section A. Chapter 579, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto
2 one new section, to be known as section 579.200, to read as
3 follows:
1 579.200. 1. A person commits the offense of
2 consumption of marijuana in a public space if he or she
3 knowingly consumes marijuana, as such term is defined in
4 Article XIV of the Constitution of Missouri, in a public
5 space that is not otherwise licensed or approved for such
6 purpose under Article XIV of the Constitution of Missouri.
7 2. The offense of consumption of marijuana in a public
8 space is a class B misdemeanor.
9 3. As used in this section, a "public space" shall be
10 a publicly maintained property or facility, including, but
11 not limited to, a street, alley, highway, park, building,
12 facility, or any other physical asset owned, leased, or
13 controlled by this state or any agency or political
14 subdivision thereof.
