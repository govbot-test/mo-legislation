SECOND REGULAR SESSION
HOUSE CONCURRENT
RESOLUTION NO. 51
103RD GENERAL ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY REPRESENTATIVE JOBE.
7402H.01I JOSEPH ENGLER, Chief Clerk
WHEREAS, our nation's veterans selflessly served our country; and
2
3 WHEREAS, one expression of gratitude for this selfless service is veterans homes,
4 which provide necessary long term care to eligible veterans; and
5
6 WHEREAS, reimbursement from the United States Department of Veterans Affairs
7 often falls short of meeting the actual expenses for providing certain high cost medications
8 and treatments to veterans residing in veterans' homes:
9
10 NOW THEREFORE BE IT RESOLVED that the members of the House of
11 Representatives of the One Hundred Third General Assembly, Second Regular Session, the
12 Senate concurring therein, hereby urge Congress to pass H.R. 1970, which would require the
13 United States Department of Veterans Affairs to provide full reimbursement to veterans'
14 homes or directly furnish certain high-cost, specialized medications for severely disabled
15 veterans; and
16
17 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Chief Clerk of the Missouri House of
18 Representatives be instructed to prepare a properly inscribed copy of this resolution for the
19 members of the Missouri congressional delegation.
