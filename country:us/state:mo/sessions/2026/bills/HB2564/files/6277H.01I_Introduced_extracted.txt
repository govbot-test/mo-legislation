AN ACT
To amend chapter 565, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to the offense of
masked intimidation, with penalty provisions.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
Section A. Chapter 565, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto one new section, to be
2 known as section 565.097, to read as follows:
565.097. 1. A person commits the offense of masked intimidation if that person
2 intentionally harasses, intimidates, or threatens any other person or group of persons
3 while hiding or concealing his or her face with a mask or any other article or device for
4 the purpose of concealing his or her identity and with the intent to place another person
5 or group of persons in reasonable fear for their physical safety.
6 2. A person who commits the offense of masked intimidation shall be guilty of a
7 class E felony for the first offense, a class D felony for the second offense, and a class C
8 felony for a third or subsequent offense.
9 3. For purposes of this section, the following terms shall mean:
10 (1) "Harass", to engage in a knowing and willful pattern of conduct directed at a
11 particular person or particular group of persons that is intended to cause that person or
12 that group of persons to reasonably fear for their safety or suffer substantial emotional
13 distress;
14 (2) "Intimidate", to willfully and substantially interfere, by threats,
15 intimidation, or coercion, with the exercise or enjoyment by any other person or
16 persons of rights secured by the constitution or laws of the United States, or of rights
17 secured by the constitution or laws of the state of Missouri, because of that person or

20 (3) "Threaten", to communicate a clear intention to cause imminent bodily

22 4. The provisions of this section shall not apply to any person or persons wearing
