Section 5:
301.552. 1. The "Missouri Motor Vehicle Commission" is hereby created and
2 shall be responsible for the licensing of all motor vehicle dealers, boat dealers, wholesale
3 motor vehicle auctions, public motor vehicle auctions, and wholesale motor vehicle
4 dealers under the provisions of sections 301.550 to 301.580 and under any current rules
5 and regulations or any rules or regulations that the commission may adopt. The
6 commission shall have no supervision, authority, or control over motor vehicle
7 manufacturers except with respect to those described under subdivision (2) of
8 subsection 3 of this section. The commission's main office shall be in Jefferson City
9 in quarters provided by the office of administration, division of facilities management,
10 design, and construction. That division shall also arrange for other needed office space
11 for the commission or its staff.
12 2. All the powers and duties of the department of revenue under sections 301.550
13 to 301.580 are transferred by type III transfer, as provided in the Omnibus State
14 Reorganization Act of 1974, to the Missouri motor vehicle commission. The director of
15 the department of revenue shall have no supervision, authority, or control over the
16 actions or decisions of the Missouri motor vehicle commission or the executive director
17 of the commission. The director of revenue, the commission, and the commission's
18 executive director shall collaborate to ensure proper administration of sections 301.550
19 to 301.580 and any other applicable laws and regulations.
20 3. The Missouri motor vehicle commission shall be comprised of nine members,
21 to be selected and appointed by the governor with the advice and consent of the senate,
22 who shall be appointed as follows:
23 (1) Four members who are licensed franchised motor vehicle dealers, of whom:
24 (a) Three are licensed motor vehicle dealers, one of whom shall be a licensed
25 recreational motor vehicle dealer; and
26 (b) One is a licensed boat dealer;
27 (2) Three members who are manufacturers, importers, or distributors engaged
28 in the business of selling motor vehicles to retail consumers in this state from a
29 dealership if the manufacturer, importer, or distributor owned the dealership and
30 initially submitted a dealer license application to the Missouri department of revenue on
31 or before August 28, 2023, provided that the license is subsequently granted, and the
32 ownership or controlling interest of such dealership is not transferred, sold, or conveyed
33 to another person or entity required to be licensed under chapter 407; and
HCS HB 3051 6
34 (3) Two members who represent the general public and have no pecuniary
35 interest in any motor vehicle or boat dealership.
36 4. Each member of the commission shall be:
37 (1) A citizen of the United States; and
38 (2) A resident of the state.
39 5. Commission members shall serve four-year terms. For the initial
40 appointments, three members shall serve two-year terms, three members shall serve
41 three-year terms, and three members shall serve four-year terms. Subsequent
42 appointments shall be for four years and members shall serve until their term expires
43 or until their successor is appointed and qualified. Vacancies shall be filled by the
44 governor, by and with the advice and consent of the senate, for the remainder of the
45 unexpired term.
46 6. The commission shall elect from among its members a chair and a secretary,
47 each of whom shall serve a one-year term. Meetings shall be held as prescribed by the
48 commission, but shall be held at least quarterly. Special meetings may be called by the
49 chair upon adequate notice provided by the executive director. To conduct business, a
50 quorum of five members of the commission shall be in attendance. Members shall
51 receive no salary or other compensation for their services as members but shall receive
52 their necessary and actual travel and related expenses incurred while engaged in the
53 discharge of their official duties.
54 7. The commission shall have the authority to adopt an official seal that shall
55 contain the official seal of the state of Missouri bordered by the inscription "Missouri
56 Motor Vehicle Commission".

74 No such matter shall be considered or voted upon unless at least one member appointed

80 a. The affirmative vote of at least five members of the commission; and

125 13. Nothing in sections 301.550 to 301.580 shall limit or restrain the enforcement
