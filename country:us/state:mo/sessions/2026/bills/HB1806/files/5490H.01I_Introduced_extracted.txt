SECOND REGULAR SESSION
HOUSE BILL NO. 1806
103RD GENERAL ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY REPRESENTATIVE SHARP (37).
5490H.01I JOSEPH ENGLER, Chief Clerk
AN ACT
To amend chapter 571, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to the offense of
unlawful use of a firearm by a minor, with penalty provisions.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
Section A. Chapter 571, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto one new section, to be
2 known as section 571.034, to read as follows:
571.034. 1. A person under eighteen years of age commits the offense of
2 unlawful use of a firearm by a minor if such person knowingly carries concealed upon
3 or about his or her person a firearm.
4 2. The offense of unlawful use of a firearm by a minor is a class D misdemeanor.
5 3. Upon conviction or a plea of guilty for an offense under this section, the court
6 shall order the immediate confiscation of the firearm. The court may issue any other
7 order allowed under section 571.095 in felony cases, and the procedures outlined in
8 section 571.095 shall apply.
