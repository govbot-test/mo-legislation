SECOND REGULAR SESSION
HOUSE BILL NO. 2582
103RD GENERAL ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY REPRESENTATIVE TITUS.
5379H.01I JOSEPH ENGLER, Chief Clerk
AN ACT
To amend chapter 208, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to MO HealthNet
eligibility.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
Section A. Chapter 208, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto one new section, to be
2 known as section 208.203, to read as follows:
208.203. 1. The MO HealthNet division shall expedite a verification for each
2 MO HealthNet benefits application that the applicant is not currently enrolled in any
3 other state's Medicaid program or in any qualified health plan offered through a health
4 benefit exchange established under the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act, 42
5 U.S.C. Section 18001 et seq. An applicant relocating from an uncooperative state may
6 be asked to provide a certificate of nonenrollment from that state.
7 2. Any applicant for MO HealthNet benefits currently enrolled in a program or
8 plan described in subsection 1 of this section may terminate his or her enrollment to
9 become eligible for MO HealthNet benefits. The division shall not approve the
10 application for MO HealthNet benefits until the termination of enrollment in the other
11 program or plan is effective.
