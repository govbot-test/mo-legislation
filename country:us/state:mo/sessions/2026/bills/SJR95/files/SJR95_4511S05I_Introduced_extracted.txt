38 by the state treasurer.

43 following order of priority:
44 (a) State personal income tax;
45 (b) State sales and use tax;
46 (c) State corporate income tax; and
47 (d) Other state-imposed taxes in an order of priority
48 to be determined by law.
49 (3) Notwithstanding the provisions of subdivision (1)
50 of this subsection to the contrary, once the taxes
51 enumerated in subdivision (2) of this subsection have been
52 eliminated, an amount not to exceed the most recent rate of
53 inflation or ten percent, whichever is less, of the annual
54 interest earned on moneys in the fund may be reinvested as
55 provided in subsection 2 of this section.
56 (4) Upon the elimination or reduction of any tax
57 enumerated in subdivision (2) of this subsection, no such
58 tax shall thereafter be enacted or be increased beyond the
59 rate as reduced pursuant to this section.
60 4. In the event the annual interest accrued by the
61 fund is insufficient to eliminate all state-imposed taxes,
62 taxes shall be eliminated in the order of priority specified
63 in subsection 3 of this section, with any remaining annual
64 interest allocated to reduce remaining state-imposed taxes
65 in the order of priority specified in subsection 3 of this
66 section.
