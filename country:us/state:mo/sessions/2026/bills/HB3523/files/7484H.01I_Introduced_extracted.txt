AN ACT
To amend chapter 217, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to the
establishment of the "Homes for Missouri Program" within the department of
corrections.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
Section A. Chapter 217, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto one new section, to be
2 known as section 217.995, to read as follows:
217.995. 1. There is hereby established within the department of corrections the
2 "Homes for Missouri Program". Notwithstanding the provisions of any other law to the
3 contrary, the director is authorized to contract with nonprofit entities for inmate work
4 involving the construction of single-family homes and other residential structures in
5 areas within the state with documented affordable housing shortages with such single-
6 family homes and other structures to be sold at below-market, cost-based prices.
7 2. The department may contract with one or more nonprofit organizations to
8 administer, manage, market, or otherwise operate the program. Any contract
9 authorized under this section shall be in compliance with state and federal law.
10 3. Participation in the program shall be voluntary and contingent upon
11 eligibility criteria established by the department.
12 4. The program shall incorporate structured vocational training and workforce
13 development components including, but not limited to:
14 (1) On the job training in residential construction trades including, but not
15 limited to, carpentry, electrical work, plumbing, masonry, framing, and roofing;
16 (2) Classroom instruction integrated with hands-on construction experience;
