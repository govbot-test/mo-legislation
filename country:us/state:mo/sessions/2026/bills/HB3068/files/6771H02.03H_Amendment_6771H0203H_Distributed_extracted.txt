4 "302.020. 1. Unless otherwise provided for by law, it shall be unlawful for any person,
5 except those expressly exempted by section 302.080, to:
6 (1) Operate any vehicle upon any highway in this state unless the person has a valid
7 license;
8 (2) Operate a motorcycle or motortricycle upon any highway of this state unless such
9 person has a valid license that shows the person has successfully passed an examination for the
10 operation of a motorcycle or motortricycle as prescribed by the director. The director may
11 indicate such upon a valid license issued to such person, or shall issue a license restricting the
12 applicant to the operation of a motorcycle or motortricycle if the actual demonstration, required
13 by section 302.173, is conducted on such vehicle;
14 (3) Authorize or knowingly permit a motorcycle or motortricycle owned by such person
15 or under such person's control to be driven upon any highway by any person whose license does
16 not indicate that the person has passed the examination for the operation of a motorcycle or
17 motortricycle or has been issued an instruction permit therefor;
18 (4) Operate a motor vehicle with an instruction permit or license issued to another
19 person.
20 2. Every person under twenty-six years of age who is operating or riding as a passenger
21 on any motorcycle or motortricycle, as defined in section 301.010, upon any highway of this
22 state shall wear protective headgear at all times the vehicle is in motion; except that, any person
23 twenty-six years of age or older operating any motorcycle or motortricycle who has been issued
24 an instruction permit shall wear protective headgear at all times the vehicle is in motion. The
25 protective headgear shall meet reasonable standards and specifications established by the
26 director. No political subdivision of this state shall impose a protective headgear requirement on
27 the operator or passenger of a motorcycle or motortricycle. No person shall be stopped,
28 inspected, or detained solely to determine compliance with this subsection, except that a person
29 may be stopped, inspected, or detained when there is a reasonable basis to suspect that a
30 violation of this subsection involves a child passenger under ten years of age.
