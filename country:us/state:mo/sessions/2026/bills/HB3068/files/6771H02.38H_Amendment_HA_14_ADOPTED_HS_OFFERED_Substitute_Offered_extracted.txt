18 determining the qualifications and fitness of all applicants for appointment to positions on the

21 which they seek to be appointed. Notice of such examinations and tests shall be given not less
22 than ten days in advance thereof by public advertisement in at least one newspaper of general
23 circulation in such city, and by posting notice in the police headquarters building. A list of those
24 qualifying in such examinations shall be established, listing those qualified in order of rank.
