SECOND REGULAR SESSION
HOUSE BILL NO. 3486
103RD GENERAL ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY REPRESENTATIVE LUCAS.
7448H.01I JOSEPH ENGLER, Chief Clerk
AN ACT
To amend chapter 227, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to the designation
of a memorial highway.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
Section A. Chapter 227, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto one new section, to be
2 known as section 227.911, to read as follows:
227.911. The portion of U.S. Highway 61 from CRD R7 School Road continuing
2 north to CRD Plattin Road in Jefferson County shall be designated the "Army PFC
3 Gary Prather Memorial Highway". The department of transportation shall erect and
4 maintain appropriate signs designating such highway, with the costs to be paid by the
5 department of transportation.
