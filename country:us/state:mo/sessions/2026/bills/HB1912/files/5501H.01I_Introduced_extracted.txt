AN ACT
To amend chapter 204, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to reorganized
common sewer districts.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
Section A. Chapter 204, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto one new section, to be
2 known as section 204.605, to read as follows:
204.605. Any reorganized common sewer district that is authorized to engage in
2 the construction, maintenance, and operation of water supply and distribution facilities
3 is hereby authorized to acquire, construct, improve or extend, maintain, and operate a
4 combined waterworks and sewerage system. Any such combined waterworks and
5 sewerage system may consist of an existing sewerage system, an existing waterworks, a
6 sewerage system to be acquired or to be constructed, or a waterworks to be acquired or
7 constructed, or any combination thereof, and may include any improvements or
8 extensions to be acquired or constructed either to an existing sewerage system or to an
9 existing waterworks or to both. Any such reorganized common sewer district desiring
10 to operate and maintain a combined waterworks and sewerage system shall adopt a
11 resolution declaring that its waterworks, whether then existing or to be acquired or
12 constructed, and its sewerage system then existing or to be acquired or constructed shall
13 thenceforth be operated and maintained as a combined waterworks and sewerage
14 system and may provide that such combined system shall include all future
15 improvements or extensions, whether to the waterworks or to the sewerage system, or
16 to both. All applicable provisions of this chapter shall apply to the construction,
17 operation, and maintenance of combined waterworks and sewerage system facilities,
