AN ACT
To repeal sections 72.401 and 72.420, RSMo, and to enact in lieu thereof two new sections relating
to county boundary commissions.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Missouri, as follows:
1 Section A. Sections 72.401 and 72.420, RSMo, are repealed
2 and two new sections enacted in lieu thereof, to be known as
3 sections 72.401 and 72.420, to read as follows:

2 sections 72.400 to 72.423 in any county with a charter form
