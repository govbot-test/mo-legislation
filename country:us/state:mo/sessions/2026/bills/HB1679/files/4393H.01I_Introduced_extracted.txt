SECOND REGULAR SESSION
HOUSE BILL NO. 1679
103RD GENERAL ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY REPRESENTATIVE SEITZ.
4393H.01I JOSEPH ENGLER, Chief Clerk
AN ACT
To amend chapter 191, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to children's access
to health care services.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
Section A. Chapter 191, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto one new section, to be
2 known as section 191.709, to read as follows:
191.709. 1. As used in this section, the following terms mean:
2 (1) "Health care provider", the same meaning given to the term in section
3 376.1350;
4 (2) "Health care service", the same meaning given to the term in section
5 376.1350;
6 (3) "Vaccination status", a child's status with regard to whether the child has
7 received a vaccination against one or more diseases.
8 2. A health care provider shall not refuse, withhold from, or deny a minor child
9 any health care service based on the child's vaccination status.
