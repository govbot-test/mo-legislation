Section 1:
SECOND REGULAR SESSION
103RD GENERAL ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY REPRESENTATIVE MATTHIESEN.
6132H.01I JOSEPH ENGLER, Chief Clerk
AN ACT
To amend chapter 210, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to licensed child
care facilities.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:

Section 2:
Section A. Chapter 210, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto one new section, to be
2 known as section 210.253, to read as follows:

Section 3:
210.253. The office of childhood shall design and develop placards that include a
2 QR code that contains a link to a facility's complaint investigation and inspection
3 reports. Complaint investigations shall appear in the search results in a timely manner
4 clearly differentiating such investigations from other inspection reports. By January 1,
5 2027, the office of childhood shall distribute such placards to all licensed child care
6 facilities. Each facility shall post such placard in a conspicuous place, with the state
7 license, at the entrance of the facility. Inspection reports, complaint investigations, and
8 related reports shall appear for a period of three years, after which a facility may
9 request such investigations and reports be removed by the department.
✔
EXPLANATION — Matter enclosed in bold-faced brackets [thus] in the above bill is not enacted and is
intended to be omitted from the law. Matter in bold-face type in the above bill is proposed language.
