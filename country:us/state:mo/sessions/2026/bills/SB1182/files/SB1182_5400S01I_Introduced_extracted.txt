SECOND REGULAR SESSION
SENATE BILL NO. 1182
103RD GENERA L ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY SENATOR TRENT.
5400S.01I KRISTINA MARTIN, Secretary
AN ACT
To repeal section 304.184, RSMo, and to enact in lieu thereof one new section relating to vehicle
weight limit exemptions.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Missouri, as follows:
1 Section A. Section 304.184, RSMo, is repealed and one new
2 section enacted in lieu thereof, to be known as section 304.184,
3 to read as follows:
1 304.184. 1. Notwithstanding any other provision of
2 law to the contrary, any truck, tractor-trailer or other
3 combination engaged in transporting solid waste, as defined
4 by section 260.200, between any city and a solid waste
5 disposal area or solid waste processing facility approved by
6 the department of natural resources or department of health
7 and senior services, may operate with a weight not to exceed
8 twenty-two thousand four hundred pounds on one axle or a
9 weight not to exceed forty-four thousand eight hundred
10 pounds on any tandem axle; but nothing in this section shall
11 be construed to permit the operation of any motor vehicle on
12 the interstate highway system in excess of the weight limits
13 imposed by federal statute; and no such truck, tractor-
14 trailer or other combination shall exceed the width and
15 length limitations provided in section 304.190.
16 2. Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the
17 contrary, a county commission may by order or ordinance

23 exceed forty-four thousand eight hundred pounds on any
