SECOND REGULAR SESSION
SENATE BILL NO. 1791
103RD GENERA L ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY SENATOR CIERPIOT.
7476S.01I KRISTINA MARTIN, Secretary
AN ACT
To amend chapter 130, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to campaign finance.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Missouri, as follows:
1 Section A. Chapter 130, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto
2 one new section, to be known as section 130.039, to read as
3 follows:
1 130.039. A candidate for a state office shall not make
2 contributions to his or her own candidate committee in
3 excess of the contribution limitation applicable to the
4 elective office sought pursuant to the Missouri Constitution
5 or statute, whichever is applicable.
