14 5. The task force shall develop a standard admission letter and a standard financial aid

16 letter shall clearly list at least the following information:
17 (1) The estimated cost of attendance including, but not limited to, the cost of tuition and
18 fees, room and board, books, and transportation for the current academic year as well as
19 estimates for each academic year that the student would need to attend to earn a degree at such
20 institution, with a disclaimer that the cost of attendance for years other than the current academic
21 year are estimates and may be subject to change;
22 (2) All federal, state, and institutional financial aid offered, with an explanation as to
23 which components will require repayment;
24 (3) Any expected student or family contribution;
25 (4) Campus-specific graduation, median borrowing, and loan default rates;
26 (5) A glossary of terms and definitions used in the standard letter; and
27 (6) Any other necessary information as determined by the task force.
28 6. Before January 1, 2027, the task force shall submit a report to the speaker of the house
