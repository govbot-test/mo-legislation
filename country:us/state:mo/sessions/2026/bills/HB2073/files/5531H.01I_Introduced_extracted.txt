AN ACT
To repeal section 301.190, RSMo, and to enact in lieu thereof one new section relating to
salvage motor vehicles.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
Section A. Section 301.190, RSMo, is repealed and one new section enacted in lieu
2 thereof, to be known as section 301.190, to read as follows:
301.190. 1. No certificate of registration of any motor vehicle or trailer, or number

71 registration of the vehicle. The certificate shall be good for the life of the motor vehicle or
