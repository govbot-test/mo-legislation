SENATE BILL NO. 1129
103RD GENERA L ASSEMBLY

3388S.01I KRISTINA MARTIN, Secretary
AN ACT
To repeal sections 571.121 and 571.230, RSMo, and to enact in lieu thereof two new sections
relating to concealed carry permits.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Missouri, as follows:
1 Section A. Sections 571.121 and 571.230, RSMo, are
2 repealed and two new sections enacted in lieu thereof, to be
3 known as sections 571.121 and 571.230, to read as follows:
1 571.121. 1. Any person issued a concealed carry
2 permit pursuant to sections 571.101 to 571.121, or a
3 concealed carry endorsement issued prior to August 28, 2013,
4 shall carry the concealed carry permit or endorsement at all
5 times the person is carrying a concealed firearm and shall
6 display the concealed carry permit and a state or federal
7 government-issued photo identification or the endorsement or
8 permit upon the request of any peace officer. Carrying and
9 display of a concealed carry permit or endorsement as
10 required under this subsection may be accomplished by the
11 carrying and display of a digital copy of such concealed
12 carry permit or endorsement, which includes but is not
13 limited to a personal photograph of the permit or
14 endorsement stored digitally on an electronic device.
15 Failure to comply with this subsection shall not be a
16 criminal offense but the concealed carry permit or
