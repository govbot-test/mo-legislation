AN ACT
To repeal sections 1.410, 1.420, 1.430, 1.440, 1.450, 1.460, 1.470, 1.480, and 1.485, RSMo, and
to enact in lieu thereof four new sections relating to firearms.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Missouri, as follows:
1 Section A. Sections 1.410, 1.420, 1.430, 1.440, 1.450,
2 1.460, 1.470, 1.480, and 1.485, RSMo, are repealed and four new
3 sections enacted in lieu thereof, to be known as sections 1.451,
4 1.461, 1.481, and 1.484, to read as follows:
1 1.451. No public officer or employee of this state or
2 any political subdivision of this state shall have the
3 authority to knowingly violate a law-abiding citizen's legal
4 rights regarding firearms, firearm accessories, or
5 ammunition. Nothing in sections 1.451 to 1.484 shall be
6 construed to prohibit Missouri officials from accepting aid
7 from federal officials or rendering aid to federal
8 officials. Nothing in sections 1.451 to 1.484 shall
9 prohibit law enforcement from working on federal task forces.

2 enforcement agency that employs a law enforcement officer

4 violate the provisions of section 1.451 or otherwise
5 knowingly deprives a law-abiding citizen of Missouri of the
6 rights or privileges ensured by the Second Amendment of the
7 Constitution of the United States or Article I, Section 23
