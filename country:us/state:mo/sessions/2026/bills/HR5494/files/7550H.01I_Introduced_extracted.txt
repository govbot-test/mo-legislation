SECOND REGULAR SESSION
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 5494
103RD GENERAL ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY REPRESENTATIVE DAVIDSON.
7550H.01I JOSEPH ENGLER, Chief Clerk
WHEREAS, the Tenth Amendment to the Constitution of the United States reserves
2 powers not delegated to the federal government to the states, and education is not enumerated
3 as a responsibility of the federal government; and
4
5 WHEREAS, the U. S. Department of Education's creation in 1980 has led to
6 significant federal overreach in education through burdensome regulations and one-size-fits-
7 all mandates; and
8
9 WHEREAS, despite an annual budget of $60 billion and another $276 billion in one-
10 time spending for COVID-19 recovery efforts, 4th and 8th grade reading scores remain
11 roughly unchanged since the early 1990s when national measurements first began; and
12
13 WHEREAS, states and local communities possess unique knowledge of their
14 students' educational needs and are best positioned to develop the curriculum and policies that
15 reflect their values and priorities; and
16
17 WHEREAS, President Trump has publicly expressed a desire to eliminate the U. S.
18 Department of Education and rightfully put the states back in charge of education; and
19
20 WHEREAS, the elimination of the U.S. Department of Education is not only a
21 constitutional necessity but will also cause taxpayer dollars to be spent more efficiently and
22 lead to better student outcomes:
23
24 NOW THEREFORE BE IT RESOLVED that the Missouri House of
25 Representatives, One Hundred Third General Assembly, Second Regular Session, hereby
26 supports federal efforts to eliminate the U.S. Department of Education and urges the United
27 States Congress to fully cooperate with these efforts; and
28

29 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Chief Clerk of the Missouri House of
30 Representatives be instructed to prepare a properly inscribed copy of this resolution for the
31 President of the United States, the Speaker of the United States House of Representatives, and
32 the Majority Leader of the United States Senate.
