SECOND REGULAR SESSION
SENATE BILL NO. 1025
103RD GENERA L ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY SENATOR MAY.
4396S.01I KRISTINA MARTIN, Secretary
AN ACT
To repeal section 192.2410, RSMo, and to enact in lieu thereof one new section relating to the
elder abuse and neglect hotline.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Missouri, as follows:
1 Section A. Section 192.2410, RSMo, is repealed and one
2 new section enacted in lieu thereof, to be known as section
3 192.2410, to read as follows:
1 192.2410. 1. A report made under section 192.2405
2 shall be made orally or in writing. It shall include, if
3 known:
4 (1) The name, age, and address of the eligible adult;
5 (2) The name and address of any person responsible for
6 care of the eligible adult;
7 (3) The nature and extent of the condition of the
8 eligible adult; and
9 (4) Other relevant information.
10 2. Reports regarding persons determined not to be
11 eligible adults as defined in section 192.2400 shall be
12 referred to the appropriate state or local authorities.
13 3. The department shall maintain a statewide toll-free
14 phone number for receipt of reports and shall operate the
15 hotline continuously for twenty-four hours a day, seven days
16 a week.
