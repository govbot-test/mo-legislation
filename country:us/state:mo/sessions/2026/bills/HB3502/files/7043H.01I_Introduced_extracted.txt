SECOND REGULAR SESSION
HOUSE BILL NO. 3502
103RD GENERAL ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY REPRESENTATIVE THOMAS.
7043H.01I JOSEPH ENGLER, Chief Clerk
AN ACT
To amend chapter 10, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to the official
professional soccer team of the state of Missouri.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
Section A. Chapter 10, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto one new section, to be
2 known as section 10.286, to read as follows:
10.286. The Kansas City Current soccer team is selected for and shall be known
2 as the official soccer team for the state of Missouri.
✔
EXPLANATION — Matter enclosed in bold-faced brackets [thus] in the above bill is not enacted and is
intended to be omitted from the law. Matter in bold-face type in the above bill is proposed language.
