Section 1:
SECOND REGULAR SESSION
103RD GENERA L ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY SENATOR FITZWATER.
4513S.01I KRISTINA MARTIN, Secretary
AN ACT
To amend chapter 142, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to motor fuel tax.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Missouri, as follows:
1 Section A. Chapter 142, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto
2 one new section, to be known as section 142.1050, to read as
3 follows:
1 142.1050. Notwithstanding any provision of law to the
2 contrary, any tax, excise, license, or fee upon, measured by
3 or with respect to the importation, receipt, manufacture,
4 storage, transportation, sale, or use of fuel used for
5 propelling motor vehicles authorized by a political
6 subdivision shall expire five years after enactment unless
7 reauthorized by a two-thirds majority vote of the people of
8 the political subdivision.
