Section 1:
SECOND REGULAR SESSION
103RD GENERA L ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY SENATOR CARTER.
5181S.01I KRISTINA MARTIN, Secretary
AN ACT
To amend chapter 595, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to crime victim
assistance.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Missouri, as follows:
1 Section A. Chapter 595, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto
2 one new section, to be known as section 595.047, to read as
3 follows:
1 595.047. 1. (1) There is hereby created in the state
2 treasury the "Missouri Crime Victims Fund", which shall
3 consist of moneys appropriated by the general assembly and
4 any gifts, contributions, grants, or other donations from
5 any public or private source. The state treasurer shall be
6 custodian of the fund. In accordance with sections 30.170
7 and 30.180, the state treasurer may approve disbursements.
8 The fund shall be a dedicated fund and, upon appropriation,
9 moneys in this fund shall be used solely as provided in this
10 section.
11 (2) Notwithstanding the provisions of section 33.080
12 to the contrary, any moneys remaining in the fund at the end
13 of the biennium shall not revert to the credit of the
14 general revenue fund.
15 (3) The state treasurer shall invest moneys in the
16 fund in the same manner as other funds are invested. Any
SB 1242 2
17 interest and moneys earned on such investments shall be
18 credited to the fund.
19 2. Moneys in the Missouri crime victims fund shall be
20 disbursed to entitlement jurisdictions, eligible entities,
21 or local governmental entities that are eligible for victim
22 assistance grants under the federal Victims of Crime Act,
23 Pub. L. 98-473, and related regulations, and such
24 jurisdictions and entities shall use such moneys for the
25 same purposes as an entitlement jurisdiction, eligible
26 entity, or local governmental entity may use victim
27 assistance grants under the federal Victims of Crime Act,
28 Pub. L. 98-473, and related regulations.
