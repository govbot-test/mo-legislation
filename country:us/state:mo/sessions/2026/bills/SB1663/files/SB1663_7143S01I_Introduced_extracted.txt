AN ACT
To repeal sections 620.580, 620.582, 620.584, 620.586, 620.588, 620.590, and 620.592, RSMo,
and to enact in lieu thereof seven new sections relating to the Missouri community
service commission.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Missouri, as follows:

2 620.588, 620.590, and 620.592, RSMo, are repealed and seven new

5 173.4012, to read as follows:

1 [620.584.] 173.4004. 1. The Missouri community
2 service commission is assigned to the department of
3 [economic development] higher education and workforce
4 development.
5 2. The commission is established to make community
6 service the common expectation and experience of all
7 Missourians with a special concentration on Missouri's young
8 people. The commission shall focus its efforts primarily on
9 issues related to education, public safety, human needs, and
10 the environment.
11 3. The commission shall work to renew the ethic of
12 civic responsibility in Missouri and to involve and enroll
13 citizens in service opportunities that benefit Missouri
14 while offering citizens skills that can be used to further
15 their own plans for education, for a career, or for
16 continuing community services. The commission shall build
17 on the existing organizational framework of state, local,
18 and community-based programs and agencies to expand full-
19 time and part-time service opportunities for all citizens,
20 but particularly Missouri's youth.

57 7. The commission shall meet at least quarterly.
