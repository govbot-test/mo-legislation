SECOND REGULAR SESSION
HOUSE BILL NO. 2576
103RD GENERAL ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY REPRESENTATIVE BLACK.
5665H.02I JOSEPH ENGLER, Chief Clerk
AN ACT
To amend chapter 227, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to the designation
of a memorial bridge.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
Section A. Chapter 227, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto one new section, to be
2 known as section 227.903, to read as follows:
227.903. The bridge on State Highway CC crossing over Interstate 44 in Webster
2 County shall be designated the "Dr Tommy Macdonnell Memorial Bridge". The
3 department of transportation shall erect and maintain appropriate signs designating
4 such bridge. Notwithstanding provisions of section 227.296, the costs shall be paid by
5 the department of transportation in recognition of the honoree's Silver Star Award.
