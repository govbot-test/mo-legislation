SECOND REGULAR SESSION
HOUSE BILL NO. 3363
103RD GENERAL ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY REPRESENTATIVE JAMISON.
6890H.02I JOSEPH ENGLER, Chief Clerk
AN ACT
To repeal section 478.385, RSMo, and to enact in lieu thereof one new section relating to
circuit judges in the seventh judicial circuit.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
Section A. Section 478.385, RSMo, is repealed and one new section enacted in lieu
2 thereof, to be known as section 478.385, to read as follows:
478.385. There shall be four circuit judges in the seventh judicial circuit [consisting
2 of the county of Clay]. Beginning fiscal year 2028, there shall be five circuit judges in the
3 seventh judicial circuit.
✔
EXPLANATION — Matter enclosed in bold-faced brackets [thus] in the above bill is not enacted and is
intended to be omitted from the law. Matter in bold-face type in the above bill is proposed language.
