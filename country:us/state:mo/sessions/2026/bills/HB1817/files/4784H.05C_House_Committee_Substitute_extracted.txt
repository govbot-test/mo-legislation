AN ACT
To amend chapters 192 and 660, RSMo, by adding thereto two new sections relating to public
assistance benefits.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
Section A. Chapters 192 and 660, RSMo, are amended by adding thereto two new
2 sections, to be known as sections 192.023 and 660.080, to read as follows:
192.023. 1. The department shall, on a monthly basis, make a report available
2 on its website that lists for each public assistance program administered by the
3 department including, but not limited to, for each city, town, or village or county with
4 more than one thousand inhabitants:
5 (1) The total number of households enrolled;
6 (2) The total number of people served;
7 (3) The average monthly benefit received per household receiving benefits; and
8 (4) The number of households that became ineligible to receive benefits.
9 2. Nothing in this section shall require the disclosure of personally identifiable
10 information. The department shall not report confidential information and use only
11 aggregate data.
660.080. 1. The department shall, on a monthly basis, make a report available
2 on its website that lists for each public assistance program administered by the
3 department including, but not limited to, the Supplemental Nutrition Assistance
4 Program, Temporary Assistance for Needy Families, and Women, Infants and Children,
5 for each city, town, or village or county with more than one thousand inhabitants:
6 (1) The total number of households enrolled;
7 (2) The total number of people served;
