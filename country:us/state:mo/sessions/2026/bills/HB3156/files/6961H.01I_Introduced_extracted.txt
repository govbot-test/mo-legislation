AN ACT
To repeal section 100.240, RSMo, and to enact in lieu thereof one new section relating to the
show-me sports investment act.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
Section A. Section 100.240, RSMo, is repealed and one new section enacted in lieu
2 thereof, to be known as section 100.240, to read as follows:
100.240. 1. This section shall be known and may be cited as the "Show-Me Sports
2 Investment Act".
3 2. The state of Missouri, acting through the department and the office of
4 administration, may, upon such terms and with reasonable consideration as it may determine,
5 subject to appropriation, expend funds for the purpose of aiding and cooperating in the
6 planning, undertaking, financing, or carrying out of an athletic and entertainment facility
7 project for which application is made to the department and approved by the director and the
8 commissioner.
9 3. As used in this section, the following terms shall mean:
10 (1) "Athletic and entertainment facility", structures, fixtures, systems, and facilities of
11 sports and entertainment venues with seating capacity of more than thirty thousand, including
12 associated parking facilities, and that the director and commissioner determine is a
13 contributing factor in the attraction or retention of sports, recreational, or entertainment
14 activities, whether professional, commercial, or private, and a primary factor in the retention
15 of a professional sports franchise in the state. An athletic and entertainment facility may
16 include a professional sports franchise's headquarters facility and training facility, regardless
17 of whether they are co-located in or adjacent to the stadium, but still located within the state.

intended to be omitted from the law. Matter in bold-face type in the above bill is proposed language.
