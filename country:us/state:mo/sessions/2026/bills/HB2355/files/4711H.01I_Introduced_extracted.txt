103RD GENERAL ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY REPRESENTATIVE JONES (88).
4711H.01I JOSEPH ENGLER, Chief Clerk
AN ACT
To amend chapter 208, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to a MO HealthNet
waiver for nutrition services.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
Section A. Chapter 208, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto one new section, to be
2 known as section 208.270, to read as follows:
208.270. 1. This section shall be known and may be cited as the "Food is
2 Medicine Act". The department of social services shall apply to the Centers for
3 Medicare and Medicaid Services of the federal Department of Health and Human
4 Services for a Section 1115 demonstration waiver to implement the "Food is Medicine"
5 program for the purpose of providing nutritional support through the MO HealthNet
6 program. The food is medicine program shall be designed to improve health outcomes
7 for MO HealthNet participants with nutrition-related chronic diseases through
8 nutrition services and to reduce the need for medical care for those participants.
9 2. Nutrition services covered under this program may include, but shall not be
10 limited to, the following:
11 (1) Case management;
12 (2) Nutrition counseling;
13 (3) Meals or pantry stocking;
14 (4) Nutrition prescriptions; and
15 (5) Grocery provisions.
EXPLANATION — Matter enclosed in bold-faced brackets [thus] in the above bill is not enacted and is
intended to be omitted from the law. Matter in bold-face type in the above bill is proposed language.
