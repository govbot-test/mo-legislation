30 If a majority of the votes cast on the proposal by the
31 qualified voters voting thereon are in favor of the
32 proposal, then the ordinance or order and any amendments
33 thereto shall be in effect. If a majority of the votes cast
34 by the qualified voters voting are opposed to the proposal,
35 then the board of directors shall have no power to impose
36 the sales tax and reduce the property tax as herein
37 authorized unless and until the board of directors shall
38 again have submitted another proposal to authorize board of
39 directors to impose the sales tax and reduce the property
40 tax under the provisions of this section and such proposal
41 is approved by a majority of the qualified voters voting
42 thereon.
43 (b) Each year in which a sales tax is imposed pursuant
44 to this section, the board of directors shall, after
45 determining its budget, within the limits set by the
46 constitution and laws of this state for the following
47 calendar year and the total property tax levy needed to
48 raise the revenues required by such budget, reduce that
49 total real and personal property tax levy in an amount
50 sufficient to decrease the total property taxes it will
51 collect by an amount equal to one hundred percent of the
52 sales tax revenue collected in the tax year for which the
53 property taxes are being levied, provided that for the first
54 three years following the adoption of the sales tax pursuant
55 to this section, the reduction made pursuant to this
56 subdivision shall be equal to thirty-three percent, sixty-
57 six percent, and one hundred percent, respectively, of the
58 sales tax revenue collected.".
