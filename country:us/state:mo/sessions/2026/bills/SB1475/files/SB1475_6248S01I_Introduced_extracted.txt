SECOND REGULAR SESSION
SENATE BILL NO. 1475
103RD GENERA L ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY SENATOR NICOLA.
6248S.01I KRISTINA MARTIN, Secretary
AN ACT
To repeal sections 137.085, 140.110, 140.160, 140.850, and 141.260, RSMo, and to enact in lieu
thereof five new sections relating to delinquent property taxes, with an emergency
clause.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Missouri, as follows:
1 Section A. Sections 137.085, 140.110, 140.160, 140.850,
2 and 141.260, RSMo, are repealed and five new sections enacted
3 in lieu thereof, to be known as sections 137.085, 140.110,
4 140.160, 140.850, and 141.260, to read as follows:
1 137.085. 1. Government lands entered or located on
2 prior to the first day of January shall be taxable for that
3 year and every year thereafter; school and swamp land and
4 lots shall become taxable whenever the county sells, conveys
5 or agrees to convey its title.
6 2. (1) Real property shall in all cases be liable for
7 the taxes thereon, and a lien is hereby vested in favor of
8 the state on all real property for all taxes thereon, which
9 lien shall accrue and become a fixed encumbrance as soon as
10 the amount of the taxes is determined by assessment and
11 levy, and said lien shall be enforced as provided by law;
12 said lien shall continue to be enforced until all taxes,
13 forfeitures, back taxes and costs shall be fully paid or the
14 land sold or released as provided by law.
