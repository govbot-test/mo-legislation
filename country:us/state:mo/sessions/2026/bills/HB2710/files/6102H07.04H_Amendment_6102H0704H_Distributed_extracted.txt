1 AMEND House Committee Substitute for House Bill No. 2710, Page 5, Section 160.524, Line
2 157, by inserting after said line the following:
3
4 "15. (1) In addition to the performance rating assigned under subsection 4 of this
5 section, the department shall develop, or contract with a private entity in accordance with the
6 provisions of chapter 34 to develop, a school climate rating for each public school district, public
7 school, and public charter school such rating shall be posted in the same manner as the
8 performance rating.
9 (2) The school climate rating shall be based on factors calculated using metrics
10 developed by the department on a scale of zero to one-hundred and reported separately as
11 distinct ratings and each factor contributing an equal twenty-five percent weight to the overall
12 school climate rating. Such factors include:
13 (a) Suspension rates and seclusion and restraint incident rates;
14 (b) A score on a district and school survey of parent satisfaction;
15 (c) A score on a district and school survey of teacher satisfaction; and
16 (d) A score on a district and school survey of student satisfaction.
17 (3) (a) The department of elementary and secondary education shall develop and
18 administer an annual web-based school climate survey to be completed by parents, teachers, and
19 students for each school district, public school, and public charter school.
20 (b) The survey shall be designed using empirically validated, nonbiased methodologies
21 and shall be constructed in consultation with a diverse group of stakeholders including, but not
22 limited to, representatives of parent organizations, educator associations, student groups,
23 community organizations, and subject matter experts.
24 (c) The survey shall measure perceptions of school safety, engagement, communication,
25 and overall learning environment, and shall be implemented in a manner that ensures anonymity,
26 protects respondent confidentiality, and promotes reliable and representative participation across
27 all demographic groups.
28 (d) The survey shall collect anonymous, nonbiased, real-time data tied to each specific
29 school district, public school, and public charter school that the state and the school may access
30 in order to study and identify actionable feedback to foster a safe, supportive, and engaging
31 learning environment in public schools."; and
32
33 Further amend said bill by amending the title, enacting clause, and intersectional references
34 accordingly.
