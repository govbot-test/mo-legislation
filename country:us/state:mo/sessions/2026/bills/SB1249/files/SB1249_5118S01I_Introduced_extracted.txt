SECOND REGULAR SESSION
SENATE BILL NO. 1249
103RD GENERA L ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY SENATOR GREGORY (15).
5118S.01I KRISTINA MARTIN, Secretary
AN ACT
To repeal section 590.100, RSMo, and to enact in lieu thereof one new section relating to peace
officer licenses.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Missouri, as follows:
1 Section A. Section 590.100, RSMo, is repealed and one new
2 section enacted in lieu thereof, to be known as section 590.100,
3 to read as follows:
1 590.100. 1. The director shall have cause to deny any
2 application for a peace officer license or entrance into a
3 basic training course when the director has knowledge that
4 would constitute cause to discipline the applicant if the
5 applicant were licensed.
6 2. The director shall have cause to deny any
7 application for a peace officer license or entrance into a
8 basic training course when the applicant had a peace officer
9 license that was permanently revoked or surrendered.
10 3. The director shall have cause to deny any
11 application for a peace officer license or entrance into a
12 basic training course when the applicant is not a citizen of
13 the United States.
14 4. When the director has knowledge of cause to deny an
15 application pursuant to this section, the director may grant
16 the application subject to probation or may deny the
17 application. The director shall notify the applicant in
18 writing of the reasons for such action and of the right to
19 appeal pursuant to this section.

and is intended to be omitted in the law.
