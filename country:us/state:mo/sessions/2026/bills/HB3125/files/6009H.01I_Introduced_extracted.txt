103RD GENERAL ASSEMBLY

6009H.01I JOSEPH ENGLER, Chief Clerk
AN ACT
To amend chapter 137, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to real property tax
exemptions.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
Section A. Chapter 137, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto one new section, to be
2 known as section 137.1062, to read as follows:
137.1062. 1. As used in this section, the following terms mean:
2 (1) "Eligible owner", an individual who:
3 (a) Has reached sixty-five years of age as of January first of the year for which
4 an exemption is granted under this section;
5 (b) Is the owner of record of the residential real property used as a homestead or
6 has a legal or equitable interest in the residential real property used as a homestead as
7 evidenced by a written instrument; and
8 (c) Is liable for the payment of real property taxes on the residential real
9 property used as a homestead;
10 (2) "Homestead", the residential real property that is used as a primary
11 residence and the adjacent real property as is reasonably necessary for use of the
12 residence as a home dwelling.
13 2. In addition to all other exemptions authorized under Article X, Section 6 of
14 the Constitution of Missouri or otherwise authorized by law, and as authorized under
15 Article X, Section 6(a) of the Constitution of Missouri, for all tax years beginning on or
16 after January 1, 2027, a homestead owned by an eligible owner shall be exempt from the
17 real property tax assessed on such homestead.
EXPLANATION — Matter enclosed in bold-faced brackets [thus] in the above bill is not enacted and is
intended to be omitted from the law. Matter in bold-face type in the above bill is proposed language.
