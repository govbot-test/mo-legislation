103RD GENERAL ASSEMBLY

7331H.01I JOSEPH ENGLER, Chief Clerk
AN ACT
To amend chapter 68, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to uses for port
authority developments.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:
Section A. Chapter 68, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto one new section, to be
2 known as section 68.330, to read as follows:
68.330. 1. A covenant agreement for incentivized development under this
2 chapter that is supported by bonds, leases, payments in lieu of taxes, or exemptions shall
3 include a provision prohibiting use of the facilities for detention, corrections, or civil
4 confinement for a period of three years from the date of the agreement.
5 2. The provisions of subsection 1 of this section shall not apply if the following
6 entities agree to an exemption:
7 (1) The port authority board, in a public hearing;
8 (2) The governing body of the municipality in which the port authority facility is
9 located; and
10 (3) The governing bodies of a majority of affected taxing jurisdictions.
11 3. A violation of subsection 1 of this section without meeting the requirements for
12 exemption under subsection 2 of this section shall result in:
13 (1) Automatic termination of any incentives;
14 (2) A clawback equal to the value of incentives already realized; and
