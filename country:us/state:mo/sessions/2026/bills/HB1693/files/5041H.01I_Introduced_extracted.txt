Section 1:
SECOND REGULAR SESSION
103RD GENERAL ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY REPRESENTATIVE CUPPS.
5041H.01I JOSEPH ENGLER, Chief Clerk
AN ACT
To amend chapter 301, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to permanent
vehicle registration.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the state of Missouri, as follows:

Section 2:
Section A. Chapter 301, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto one new section, to be
2 known as section 301.148, to read as follows:

Section 3:
301.148. 1. Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary,
2 nonapportioned motor vehicles eligible for registration under this chapter may, at the
3 option of the registrant, be registered permanently upon the payment of seven times the
4 applicable fees required to register the vehicle annually and presentation of all
5 documentation otherwise required by law to register the vehicle annually.
6 2. The permanent plate or plates and registration fees are vehicle specific. The
7 plate or plates and the registration fees paid are nontransferable and nonrefundable,
8 except those covered under the provisions of section 301.442. Upon the transfer of the
9 title to any vehicle registered in accordance with this section, the registration shall be
10 canceled and the license plates issued therefor shall be returned to the director of
11 revenue.
12 3. As used in this section, the term "fees" shall include:
13 (1) Fees for registering the vehicle in accordance with this chapter;
14 (2) Emblem use authorization fees, contributions, or donations as specified in
15 this chapter for the issuance of special license plates, which shall be paid to the
16 organization issuing the emblem use authorization statement for the plate or plates
17 unless otherwise specified by law; and
EXPLANATION — Matter enclosed in bold-faced brackets [thus] in the above bill is not enacted and is
intended to be omitted from the law. Matter in bold-face type in the above bill is proposed language.
