Section 1:
SECOND REGULAR SESSION
103RD GENERA L ASSEMBLY
INTRODUCED BY SENATOR NICOLA.
4230S.01I KRISTINA MARTIN, Secretary
AN ACT
To amend chapter 195, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to the over-the-counter
purchase of certain medications.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Missouri, as follows:
1 Section A. Chapter 195, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto
2 one new section, to be known as section 195.1000, to read as
3 follows:
1 195.1000. Notwithstanding any other provision of law
2 to the contrary, ivermectin and hydroxychloroquine tablets
3 suitable for human use may be sold or purchased as over-the-
4 counter medications in this state without a prescription or
5 consultation with a pharmacist or other health care
6 professional.
